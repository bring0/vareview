Eisenhower VA Medical Center has been serving veterans. (more history) Today, we proudly serve our nation’s veterans with excellent health care as part of the VA Eastern Kansas Health Care System (VAEKHCS). We consider it our privilege to serve your health care needs in any way we can.

VA Eastern Kansas Health Care System’s primary service area consists of 39 counties in Kansas and Missouri. Veteran population in these counties totals over 104,000. VAEKHCS provides care to approximately 36,000 veterans.

Community-Based Outpatient Clinics

In addition to our main facilities in Topeka and Leavenworth, we offer services in 9 community based outpatient clinics. These clinics are located in –

Kansas

  • Chanute
  • Emporia
  • Fort Scott
  • Garnett
  • Junction City
  • Kansas City
  • Lawrence
  • Seneca

Missouri

  • St. Joseph

Mission

To provide accessible, courteous, comprehensive, and quality health care to veterans in an environment of excellence.

Vision

Promote the health and wellness of the veteran population we serve.

Achieve distinction as a quality patient-driven health care system that provides the full range of medical, behavioral, rehabilitative, and preventive services to veterans and others.

Improve our clinical care through research, education, and creative administration to become a model for the future.

Core Values

Integrity, Commitment, Advocacy, Respect, and Excellence.
