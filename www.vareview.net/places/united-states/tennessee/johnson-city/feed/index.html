Since 1903, James H. Quillen VA Medical Center has been improving the health of the men and women who have so proudly served our nation. We consider it our privilege to serve your health care needs in any way we can. Services are available to more than 170,000 veterans living in a 41-county area of Tennessee, Virginia, and Kentucky.

Mission

Honor and serve America’s Veterans by providing exceptional healthcare that is patient centered and preferred by Veterans.

Vision

We will be the healthcare system of choice where patients choose to receive care and employees are proud to work.

To accomplish this:

  1. We promise to provide exceptional quality and safety
  2. We promise to provide appropriate and timely access
  3. We promise to exceed Veterans’ expectations
  4. We promise to work collaboratively to deliver innovative solutions
  5. We promise to provide quality education to future healthcare providers
  6. We promise to research the answers for tomorrow
  7. We promise to provide excellent healthcare value for Veterans
