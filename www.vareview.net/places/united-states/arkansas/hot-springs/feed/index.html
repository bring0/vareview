  • Primary care services for veterans in south west Arkansas area.
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Behavioral Health services including individual, group and family counseling, and programs such as QuitSmart Smoking Cessation.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the Central Arkansas Veterans Healthcare System.
  • Women’s Health
  • Laboratory: Blood drawing services available.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.
  • Digital Radiology service.
  • Expanded Telehealth services to include Diabetic Teleetinal lImaging.
  • Nutritional Support include the MOV program to assist with weight management.

Then merge onto US – 70 W / Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Expy via Exit 6 toward US-270 Glenwood / Mount Ida for 4.7 miles. Take Exit 9, US-270W. Turn right onto Malvern Ave/US-270-BR. Travel 1.5 miles. Turn left onto Sawtooth Oak St. Clinic address is 177 Sawtooth Oak St.
