Directions

The VA Community Based Outpatient Clinic’s new location is in the same building as the New Hampshire Department of Health and Human Services, just behind the Ham Ice Arena which is located at the intersection of West Main Street and Hobbs Street off of Route 16 in Conway village. It is on the third floor of the Thibodeau Building.

From the South: turn right at the blinking light in front of Kennett High School (just after the Kancamagus Highway intersection (Route 112)).

  • Traveling north on Route 16: right on West Main Street, right on Hobbs before the Ham Ice Arena, building is on the left.
  • Traveling north on Route 153: left on West Main Street, left on Hobbs after the Ham Ice Arean, building is on the left.

From the North: turn left just after the Conway Village Fire Department. Hobbs Street is a left turn about 1/4 mile down.

  • Traveling south on Route 16: left on West Main Street, left on Hobbs after the Ham Ice Arean, building is on the left.

From the West: Route 25 east, left on Route 16, follow directions from the south.

From the East: Route 113 east, left on West Main Street, left on Hobbs after the Ham Ice Area, building is on the left.
