The Galesburg Outpatient Clinic is an extension of the Iowa City VA Health Care System. The clinic is located in Galesburg, Illinois. The clinic operates like a doctor’s office, NOT an emergency center, providing primary care, preventative health, and wellness services. A specific healthcare team is assigned to address your healthcare needs. You may be referred to the Iowa City VA Health Care System for specialty treatment. It is required that you call ahead for an appointment.

The Clinic also provides X-ray, EKG, lab, and pharmacy services. Patient education includes Substance Abuse Counseling, Stop Smoking Classes, and Nutrition Counseling.

Driving Directions

The Galesburg Outpatient Clinic is located at 310 Home Boulevard, Galesburg, Illinois, 61401.
