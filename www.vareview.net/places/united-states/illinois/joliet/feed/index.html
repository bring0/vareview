For more information click here.

Features

  • Primary care services for veterans in Will county and surrounding suburbs.
  • Behavioral Health services including individual, and group counseling.
  • Women’s Health
  • Tele-Speech Services
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the Edward Hines Jr. VA Hospital.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.
  • Laboratory: Blood drawing services available.

Parking

  • Free on premises

Hours

Monday – Thursday, 7:30 a.m. – 6:30 p.m. and Friday, 7:30 a.m. – 4:30 p.m.
