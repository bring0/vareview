Call Dial-a-Ride, at (503) 861-7433 to make arrangements in advance. The fee is reduced for the disabled and senior citizens.

It is an outreach clinic designed to bring VA healthcare services closer to Veterans living in Josephine County and reside in remote areas. The Grants Pass West VA is full service clinic and currently provides primary care services for chronic medical conditions including blood draws, limited health screenings, and mental health services.

Grants Pass Community Based Outpatient Clinic
1877 Williams Hwy, Hwy 238
Grants Pass, OR  97527(541) 955-5551 Fax (541) 955-7171
