Phone: 800-865-3384
Fax: (617) 376-3058

Practice Hours:
7:30 am – 4:00pm, Mon-Fri

NOTE: This is a Primary Care facility only.
No urgent care, mental health or pharmacy services are available at this location.

Located on the ground level and co-located with Manet Healthcare the VA Community Based Outpatient Clinic in Quincy is centrally located in Downtown Quincy.

The clinic, which opened in March 2015, is designed to serve those veterans living on the South Shore, in Norfolk County and all veterans south of Boston. Equipped with free parking and public transportation, this VA Boston Healthcare System affiliate will assist veterans not wanting to travel into Boston.

SERVICES:
Services provided and arranged for through the Clinic

– Primary Care
– Labs

ADVANTAGES

Â – Veterans will have one primary care doctor to coordinate care on an ongoing basis.
– Veterans can receive primary medical care closer to home and don’t have to travel to one of the three main campuses.
– Veterans will still be eligible for specialty outpatient care, inpatient care and all other entitlements through the Boston VA Healthcare System.

HOW TO ENROLL

In person or just complete an application and mail it in to the Boston VA Medical Center, Patient Services (136), 150 South Huntington Avenue, Boston, MA 02130. You may also enroll on the Internet at: https://www.1010ez.med.va.gov/sec/vha/1010ez/.
