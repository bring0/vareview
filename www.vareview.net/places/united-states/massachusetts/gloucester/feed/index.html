Edith Nourse Rogers Memorial Veterans Hospital (Bedford VA) is excited to announce the upcoming opening of the expanded Community Based Outpatient Clinic (CBOC) in Gloucester at 199 Main Street, June 2016 in order to serve Veterans better than they have ever been served!

Last Day at Addison Gilbert,  First Day at 199 Main Street

The last day of visits at Addison Gilbert will be June 9, 2016. The new clinic at 199 Main Street is expected to start seeing Veterans on June 13, 2016.

Our brand new, larger location will allow us to serve Cape Ann/Gloucester area Veterans better than they have ever been served!

Veterans with appointments scheduled during the transition will be contacted by VA if any changes are necessary.

Veterans with questions about existing appointments during this transition may call 781-687-4835.

For more information about the Ribbon Cutting Ceremony call 781-687-3486
For questions regarding existing appointments June 9 – 13, call 781-687-4835
To make or reschedule an appointment, call 978-282-0676

The Gloucester Outpatient Clinic is one of three satellite clinics of the Bedford VA. At the Gloucester clinic we provide compassionate healthcare  to eligible veterans in the Cape Ann /Gloucester area.

Services Provided

  • Primary Care
  • Mental Health Services
  • Lab Blood Drawing
  • For Mental Health services by scheduled appointment, you may call 781-687-2363 For Urgent care, after hours call 781-275-7500.
  • Prescription information is available by calling 1-800-838-6331.
  • Emergency services are not available. Dial 911
