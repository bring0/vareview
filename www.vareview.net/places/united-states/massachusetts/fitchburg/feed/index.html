We began seeing patients in our new location on July 21, 2015.

We are now located at:

881 Main Street , Fitchburg, MA 01420

The parking lot is at the corner of Academy and Elm Street on the backside of the Philip J. Philbin Federal Building (Post Office/Social Security) by the Fitchburg Art Museum.

To schedule appointments, call the VA Central Western Mass Call Center at 1-800-893-1522.

Find us on Facebook. You don’t need your own Facebook account to see our page.

Fitchburg Community Based Outpatient Clinic Mission

Provide compassionate health care services in primary care and mental health to eligible Veterans residing in the greater Fitchburg area. The staff of the clinic offers medical care and treatment, gives referrals to various specialty clinics and, as needed, arranges admissions to services at VA Medical Centers. The clinic is staffed by a physician, two nurse practitioners, a registered nurse, a licensed practical nurse, and a patient service assistant.
SERVICES PROVIDED:

Primary Care
Mental Health Services
Lab Blood Drawing

Directions (On site parking is available):

The Fitchburg VA Outpatient Clinic is located on the 1st floor in the rear of the Philip J. Philbin Federal  Building, with parking and entrance in the rear of the building. The physical address is 881 Main Street Fitchburg, MA 01420.

From the East: Take Route 2W to exit 31B. Take ramp right for Route 12 North toward Fitchburg. Turn right over bridge to stay on Water Street. At the traffic light take a left onto Route 2A/Main Street. Turn right onto Academy Street and then left onto “access road” to park.

From the West: Take Route 2E to exit 28. Take ramp right for Route 31 toward Fitchburg/Princeton. Turn left onto Route 31N/Princeton Rd. Bear right onto Route 2A/Route 31/Westminster Street. At roundabout, take 3rd exit to continue on River Street. River Street turns right and becomes Main Street. At light, turn left onto Academy Street and then left onto “access road” to park.
