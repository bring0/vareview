The staff of the Clinic offers medical care and treatment, gives referrals to various specialty clinics and, as needed arranges admissions to services at VA Medical Centers. The clinic is staffed by a physician, two nurse practitioners, a registered nurse, a licensed practical nurse, and a patient service assistant.

Services Provided

  • Primary Care
  • Mental Health Service
  • Lab Blood Drawing
  • Call 1-800-838-6331 for information on prescriptions.

Directions

From 495 North, take Route 110/Rt 113/River Street exit (Exit 49). Merge onto River St. River Street becomes Washington Square. The CBOC is on the right hand side across from the park house.
