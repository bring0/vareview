Overview

The 80,742 sq. ft. Wilmington Health Care Center opened in March of 2013 to provide exceptional health care to Veterans in the Wilmington and Brunswick communities and throughout coastal Carolina.

Health care is delivered to our Veterans through engaged, collaborative teams called Patient Aligned Care Teams.  These teams focus on whole-person care and life-long health and wellness.  Veterans are the center of the care teamthat includes their family members, caregivers, health care professionals, primary care provider, nurses and an administrative clerk.  When additional services are needed to meet their goals and needs, other care team members will be added.

Clinical & Specialty Services

  • Primary Care, Women’s Health, Mental Health
  • Procedures: Dermatology, Urology, Podiatry
  • Imaging Services:  Radiology, Ultrasound, CT Scan, Bone Density, Magnetic Resonance Imaging and Mammography
  • Medical Specialties include:  Cardiology, Dermatology, Infectious Disease, Urology
  • Other Services include:  Dental, Audiology and Speech Pathology, Eye Clinic, Physical and Occupational Therapy, Podiatry
  • The Wilmington Health care Center DOES NOT have an Emergency Department or Urgent care Clinic

Volunteer or Donate

Thinking of volunteering? We need YOU.

Volunteers are important members of our health care team and we appreciate their time, talents, and energy. Every day they help us provide the highest quality of care to the men and women who served our country.
