A 10,000 Square foot clinic is close to home for Veterans living in Wayne, Duplin, Lenoir, Sampson and Harnett Counties.  The clinic is the first to be built using Green standards and the first in VISN 6 to be designed for the Patient Aligned Care Team (PACT) model of health care.  Veterans have access to primary care, mental health, women’s care, lab services, tele-health, tele-retinal care and secure messaging.  Our Seymour Johnson AFB partners will offer access to radiology, physical therapy and an anticoagulation (Coumadin) clinic.
