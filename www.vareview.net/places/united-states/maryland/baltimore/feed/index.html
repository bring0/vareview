Directions to the Loch Raven VA Outpatient Clinic:

To reach the Loch Raven VA Outpatient Clinic, proceed as follows:

From points Northwest of Baltimore: Take 83 South to Cold Spring Lane and exit onto Cold Spring Lane heading east. Take Cold Spring Lane to Loch Raven Boulevard and turn right onto Loch Raven Boulevard. After passing the intersection with Argonne drive and the entrance for the Loch Raven VA Community Living & Rehabilitation Center, turn right on The Alameda. The entrance to the Loch Raven VA Outpatient Clinic is the first entrance on the right off of The Alameda.

From points Northeast of Baltimore: Take 95 South to 695 West towards Towson. Take the exit for Loch Raven Boulevard heading South off of 695. Go approximately five miles on Loch Raven Boulevard. After passing the intersection with Argonne drive and the entrance for the Loch Raven VA Community Living & Rehabilitation Center, turn right on The Alameda. The entrance to the Loch Raven VA Outpatient Clinic is the first entrance on the right off of The Alameda.

From points East of Baltimore: Take 695 North towards Towson. Take the exit for Loch Raven Boulevard heading South off of 695. Go approximately five miles on Loch Raven Boulevard. After passing the intersection with Argonne drive and the entrance for the Loch Raven VA Community Living & Rehabilitation Center, turn right on The Alameda. The entrance to the Loch Raven VA Outpatient Clinic is the first entrance on the right off of The Alameda.

From points South of Baltimore: Take 95 North to 395 (downtown Baltimore) and turn right onto Conway Street. Take Conway Street to Charles Street and turn left onto Charles Street. Take Charles Street through the City to 33rd Street and turn right onto 33rd Street. Take 33rd Street to Loch Raven Boulevard and turn left onto Loch Raven Boulevard. Take Loch Raven Boulevard to The Alameda and turn left onto the The Alameda. The entrance to the Loch Raven VA Outpatient Clinic is the first entrance on the right off of The Alameda.

From points West of Baltimore: Take 695 South toward Washington and exit onto 95 North toward Baltimore. Take 95 North to 395 (downtown Baltimore) and turn right onto Conway Street. Take Conway Street to Charles Street and turn left onto Charles Street. Take Charles Street through the City to 33rd Street and turn right onto 33rd Street. Take 33rd Street to Loch Raven Boulevard and turn left onto Loch Raven Boulevard. Take Loch Raven Boulevard to The Alameda and turn left onto the The Alameda. The entrance to the Loch Raven VA Outpatient Clinic is the first entrance on the right off of The Alameda.

Parking:  There is free parking available in front of the clinic, including designated spaces for handicapped parking.  Additionally, free valet parking is available for all outpatients and is accessible from the front of the clinic. Veteran patients are reminded to lock their vehicles when they arrive for their clinic appointments. The Department of Veterans Affairs cannot assume responsibility for the theft of or damage to any car on VA property.
