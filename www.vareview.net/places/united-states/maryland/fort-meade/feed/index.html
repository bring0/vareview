Directions to the Fort Meade VA Outpatient Clinic:

Take Interstate I-95 or I-295 to Maryland Route 175 East towards Fort Meade.  Follow Route 175 East to the Reece Road gate for Fort Meade (on the right).  At the Reece Road gate, Veteran patients will need to show a VA appointment letter, a government issued ID, and a VA ID card if available. After passing through the Reece Road gate, take an immediate left onto Ernie Pyle Street. Continue straight on Ernie Pyle Street through a red light at Mapes Road and a stop sign at Llewellyn Avenue. After crossing Llewellyn Avenue and after passing the Kimbrough Ambulatory Care Center, make a right onto 5th Street.  The Fort Meade VA Outpatient Clinic is located on the right at 2479 5th Street.

Parking:

There is free parking available just past clinic on the left side of the building in a VA Parking Lot. Please lock your vehicle when you arrive for a clinic appointment. The Department of Veterans Affairs cannot assume responsibility for the theft of or damage to any car on VA property.
