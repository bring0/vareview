As the newest outpatient clinic in Maine, it has rapidly grown to serve 700 veterans. First of the Capital Asset Realignment for Enhanced Services (CARES) clinics to be implemented in Maine, Lincoln CBOC has a full complement of VA staff providing physical, mental health and administrative services.

Services Provided:

Primary Care
Lab
Telehealth services for mental health/wound care/pain management/MOVE/pharmacy
