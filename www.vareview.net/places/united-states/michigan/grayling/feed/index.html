  • Primary Care services for veterans in the Grayling area.
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the VA Medical Centers in Saginaw, Detroit, Battle Creek and Ann Arbor.
  • Mental Health services including medication management, individual and group counseling such as Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD).
  • Tele-Health
  • Laboratory: Blood drawing services available.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.
  • Health Promotion

Registration and Enrollment:  Pre-registration at this clinic is required.  For eligibity questions or a registration packet please call 1-800-406-5143, extension 13124.

Parking

  • Free on premises

Hours

  • Monday – Friday
    8:00 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.
  • Closed Federal Holidays

Lab Hours

  • 8:00 a.m. – 2:30 p.m.

Directions

From the NORTH:

  1. Take I-75 South to the EXIT 259 (I-75-BL/M-93)
  2. Turn right onto I-75-BL/Hartwick Pines Rd/M-93
  3. Go about 0.5 miles and the Grayling CBOC is on the left.

From the SOUTH:

  1.  Take I-75 North to the M-93/Hartwick Pines Rd exit (EXIT 259)
  2. Turn left onto I-75-BL/Hartwick Pines Rd/M-93
  3. Go about 0.8 miles and the Grayling CBOC is on the left

From the WEST (via M-72):

  1.  Take M-72 SE & turn left onto N I 75 Business Loop/I-75-BL/M-93
  2. Follow I-75-BL/M-93 for 2.6 miles
  3. Turn right onto I-75-BL/Hartwick Pines Rd/M-93 & travel 1.1 miles
  4. Grayling CBOC will be on your right.

From the EAST:

  1. Make your way to I-75 and then follow the directions from the North or South.
