The Lansing VA Community Based Outpatient Clinic offers primary medical and mental health services. Once eligibility has been determined, each patient enrolling for care at the Outpatient Clinic is assigned to a primary care provider and team, who provides continuous and coordinated care.

The Clinic is not a walk-in Clinic and appointments are necessary.
