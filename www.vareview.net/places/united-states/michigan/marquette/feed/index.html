  • Primary Care services for veterans.
  • Behavioral Health services available.
  • Handicapped accessible.
  • Personal care.  Highly qualified primary care providers.  Specialty care referrals to the Oscar G. Johnson Medical Center in Iron Mountain, Michigan.
  • Laboratory:  Blood drawing services available.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Driving Directions

  • From the East:  Follow US-41/M-28W.  Turn right onto S MCCLELLAN AVE. Turn right onto W FAIR AVE.
  • From the West:  Follow US-41 E/MI-28 E. Turn left onto S MCCLELLAN AVE.  Turn right onto W FAIR AVE.
  • From the South:  Follow US-41.  Turn SLIGHT LEFT onto US-41 W/MI-28 W.  Turn right onto S MCCLELLAN AVE. Turn right onto W FAIR AVE.

Parking

  • Free on premises. Handicapped parking is available in front of the clinic.
