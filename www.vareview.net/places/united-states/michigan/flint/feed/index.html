  • VA Ann Arbor Healthcare System’s Flint Community-based Outpatient Clinic operates by appointment only.
  • For after-hour assistance please contact the Ann Arbor Medical Center at (734) 769-7100, or the Saginaw VA Medical Center at (989) 497-2500
  • Laboratory Services: Please contact the clinic to arrange an appointment.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions are processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Driving Directions

  1. Take US 23 NORTH
  2. US 23 becomes 23/75 NORTH
  3. Take Exit 117B (MILLER ROAD)
  4. Go left on MILLER ROAD
  5. Go right at WEST LENNON ROAD
  6. Go right at SOUTH LINDEN ROAD

Parking

  • Free on premises
