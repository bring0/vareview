  • Primary Care services for veterans.
  • Behavioral Health services.
  • Handicapped accessible.
  • Personal Care:  Highly qualified care for veterans.  Specialty care referrals to the Oscar G. Johnson Medical Center in Iron Mountain, Michigan.
  • Laboratory:  Blood drawing services available.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Driving Directions

  • From the east:  Once in Ironwood, follow US 2 (Cloverland Drive) and turn left into the drive across from the fair grounds. Look for the log building.
  • From the west:  Once in Ironwood, follow US 2 (Cloverland Drive) and turn right in the drive across from the fair grounds. Look for the log building.

Parking

  • Free on premises
