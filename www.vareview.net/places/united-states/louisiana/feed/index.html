For prescription refills, call the telephone ordering system using a touchtone phone by dialing 1-800-935-8387.  My HealtheVet users can also order prescriptions online at www.myhealth.va.gov.  Patients on long-term medications are asked to use one of these automated options for medication renewal.  Please reorder your medications at least 2 weeks before the date you will run out. A pharmacist is available on site for consultation and for processing of urgent, short-term medications.
