Places in Reserve, Louisiana, United States | VA Review http://www.vareview.net Tue, 02 Aug 2016 15:02:43 +0000 en-US hourly 1 St. John VA Outpatient Clinic http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/louisiana/reserve/louisiana2/st-john-va-outpatient-clinic/ http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/louisiana/reserve/louisiana2/st-john-va-outpatient-clinic/#reviews Thu, 07 Jul 2016 23:40:58 +0000 http://www.vareview.net/?post_type=gd_place&p=406 read more]]> The St. John Community Based Outpatient Clinic offers primary care and mental health services for Veterans.  The clinic is located next to the Louisiana War Veterans Home at 4004 West Airline Highway in Reserve.

For prescription refills, call the telephone ordering system using a touchtone phone by dialing 1-800-935-8387.  My HealtheVet users can also order prescriptions online at www.myhealth.va.gov.  Patients on long-term medications are asked to use one of these automated options for medication renewal.  Please reorder your medications at least 2 weeks before the date you will run out. A pharmacist is available on site for consultation and for processing of urgent, short-term medications.
