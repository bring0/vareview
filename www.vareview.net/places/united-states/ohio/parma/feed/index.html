Driving Directions

From the West and I-71:

Head East on I-480. Take exit 13 for Tiedeman Rd toward Brooklyn. Turn right onto Tiedeman Rd. Take the 2nd left onto Brookpark Rd. Clinic will be on the right side of the road.

From the East and I-77:

Head West on I-480. Take exit 15 for Ridge Rd toward US-42. Turn left onto Ridge Rd. Take the 1st right onto Brookpark Rd. Clinic will be on the left side of the road.
