Driving Directions

From the North:

I-71 South. Take the US-30 exit. Merge onto Route 30 West toward Mansfield. Take the Trimble Rd. exit. Turn left onto North Trimble Rd. Continue on North Trimble Road (becomes South Trimble Road after Park Ave. W). The David F. Winder Department of Veterans Affairs CBOC is located on the left (east) side of the street.

From the South:

I-71 North. Take the OH-97 exit. Turn left onto OH-97 West. Turn right onto US-42 North/Lexington Ave. Turn right onto South Trimble Road. The David F. Winder Department of Veterans Affairs CBOC is located on the right (east) side of the street.
