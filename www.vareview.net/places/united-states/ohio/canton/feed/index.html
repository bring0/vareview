Driving Directions

From the South:

77 South to Sixth St SW. Turn Left (East) onto Sixth staying in the right hand lane. Go approximately 1 mile to Market Ave. Turn Right (South) onto Market. The clinic is one block on the right.

From the North:

I-77 Northbound. East on US 30 to Cherry Avenue bearing right when you exit the ramp. Head north on Cherry to the first traffic light at Eleventh. Turn left (west) onto Eleventh. At the first intersection (about 1/2 mile) turn right (north) onto Market. The clinic is on Market Ave about 1/2 mile north on the left (west) side of the street.

From the East / West:

US 30 to Cherry Avenue bearing right when you exit the ramp. Head north on Cherry to the first traffic light at Eleventh. Turn left (west) onto Eleventh.
