Places in Santa Fe, New Mexico, United States | VA Review http://www.vareview.net Tue, 02 Aug 2016 15:02:43 +0000 en-US hourly 1 Santa Fe Clinic http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/new-mexico/santa-fe/outpatient-clinic/santa-fe-clinic/ http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/new-mexico/santa-fe/outpatient-clinic/santa-fe-clinic/#reviews Thu, 14 Jul 2016 18:54:58 +0000 http://www.vareview.net/?post_type=gd_place&p=691 read more]]> The Santa Fe Community Based Outpatient Clinic (CBOC) is designed to deliver primary care services to eligible Veterans.

Services and Scope of Care:

  • Blood Draws (by appt.only)
  • EKGs (Electrocardiograms)
  • Evaluations by Doctors
  • Immunizations
  • Routine Lab Tests

Eligibility:  For information about enrollment in the Santa Fe CBOC call Eligibility and Enrollment at (505) 265-1711, Ext. 2741.

  • Application for Care can be faxed to:  (505) 256-2808

Co-Pay:  Co-Pay may apply.
Other Pertinent Information:

  • For appointments, call the Clinic at (505) 986-8645.
  • To assist in your care and treatment, please bring all of your medications to your first appointment.
  • Please come to your new patient appointment 30 minutes early for check-in.

Emergency Care:  No emergency care is available at this facility. For after hours assistance, call 1-866-862-7863 or for emergency care call 911.
