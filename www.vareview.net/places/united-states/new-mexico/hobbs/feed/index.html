Places in Hobbs, New Mexico, United States | VA Review http://www.vareview.net Tue, 02 Aug 2016 15:02:43 +0000 en-US hourly 1 Hobbs Clinic http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/new-mexico/hobbs/outpatient-clinic/hobbs-clinic/ http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/new-mexico/hobbs/outpatient-clinic/hobbs-clinic/#reviews Thu, 14 Jul 2016 18:49:59 +0000 http://www.vareview.net/?post_type=gd_place&p=686 read more]]> The Hobbs Community Based Outpatient Clinic is designed to bring primary care services to eligible Veterans living in the following counties:  Lea, Gaines and Andrews.

Services and Scope of Care:

  • Health screening
  • Health promotion
  • Health maintenance
  • Education
  • Management of acute and chronic diseases
  • Mental Health
  • Women’s Health
  • Telehealth

Eligibility:

  • For information about enrollment in the Hobbs Community Based Clinic call Eligibility and Enrollment at 432-263-7361, ext. 7347 or 1-800-472-1365 Ext. 7347.

Co-Pay:

  • Co-Pay may apply.

Other Pertinent Information:

  • For appointments, call your PACT Team:  (575) 391-0354, Team 1 Ext. 2291
  • To assist in your care and treatment, please bring all of your medications to your first appointment.

Emergency Care:

  • No emergency care is available at this facility.  For after hours emergency care call 911.
