Services and Scope of Care:

  • Evaluations by Doctors
  • EKGs (Electrocardiograms)
  • Blood Draws (by appt. only)
  • Immunizations
  • Nurse Clinics
  • Routine Lab Tests
  • Social Work Services
  • Tele Health

 

Location:  3605 English Road, Farmington, NM  87402
Eligibility:  For information about enrollment in the Farmington CBOC call Eligibility and Enrollment at (505) 265-1711, Ext. 2741.

  • Application for Care can be faxed to:  (505) 256-2808

Co-Pay:  Co-Pay may apply.

Other Pertinent Information:

  • For appointments, call call the Clinic at (505) 326-4383/4384.
  • To assist in your care and treatment, please bring all of your medications to your first appointment.

Emergency Care:   No emergency care is available at this facility.  For after hours assistance, call 1-866-862-7863 or for emergency care call 911.
