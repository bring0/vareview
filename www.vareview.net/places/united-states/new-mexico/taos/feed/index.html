Services and Scope of Care:

  • Blood draws
  • EKGs (electrocardiograms)
  • Evaluations by providers
  • Routine lab tests
  • Telemedicine, including teleretinal screening, teledermatology, telemental health and telenutrition.

Location:  1353 Paseo Del Pueblo Sur, Taos, NM  87571

Health Net Federal Service customer service phone number:  1-800-621-6499

Eligibility:  For information about enrollment in the Taos VA Outpatient Clinic, please call Eligibility and Enrollment at (505) 265-1711, ext. 2741.

Emergency Care: No emergency care is available at this facility. For after hours assistance, call 1-866-862-7863 or for emergency care call 911.
