The Clovis VA Community Based Outpatient Clinic (CBOC) is designed to deliver primary care services to eligible Veterans living in Curry County and outlying areas.

Services and Scope of Care:

  • Health screening
  • Health promotion
  • Health maintenance
  • Education
  • Management of acute and chronic diseases
  • Routine/urgent care

Hours of Operation:

  • Monday through Friday 7:00am – 3:30pm
    (closed Saturday, Sunday and Federal holidays)

Location:

  • 921 East Llano Estacado, Clovis, NM. 88101

Eligibility:

  • For information about enrollment in the Clovis Community Based Outpatient Clinic call Eligibility and Enrollment at
    (800) 687-8262, Extension: 7215, 7825, 7653

Co-Pay:

  • Co-Pay may apply

Other Pertinent Information:

  • No walk-in appointments are available at this facility. For appointments, call Telephone Linked Care (TLC) at the Amarillo VA Health Care System (806) 355-9703 or 1-800-687-8262.
    To assist in your care and treatment, please bring all of your medications to your first appointment.

Emergency Care:

  • No emergency care is available at this facility.
    For after hours emergency care call 911.
