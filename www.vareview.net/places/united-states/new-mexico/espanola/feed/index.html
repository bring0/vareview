Places in Española, New Mexico, United States | VA Review http://www.vareview.net Tue, 02 Aug 2016 15:02:43 +0000 en-US hourly 1 Espanola Clinic http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/new-mexico/espanola/outpatient-clinic/espanola-clinic/ http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/new-mexico/espanola/outpatient-clinic/espanola-clinic/#reviews Thu, 14 Jul 2016 18:46:58 +0000 http://www.vareview.net/?post_type=gd_place&p=683 read more]]> The Espanola Contract Community Based Outpatient Clinic (CBOC) is designed to deliver primary care services to eligible veterans living in Rio Arriba County and outlying areas.

Services and Scope of Care:

  • Blood Draws
  • EKGs (Electrocardiograms)
  • Evaluations by Doctors
  • Routine Lab Tests
  • Social Work Services

Location:  105 S. Coronado Ave., Espanola, NM 87532

Health Net Federal Service customer service phone number is 1-800-621-6499

Eligibility:

For information about enrollment in the Espanola Contract CBOC call Eligibility and Enrollment at (505) 265-1711, Ext. 2741.

  • Application for Care can be faxed to:  (505) 256-2808

Co-Pay:  Co-Pay may apply.

Other Pertinent Information:

  • For appointments, call the clinic at (505) 367-4213.
  • To assist in your care and treatment, please bring all of your medications to your first appointment.

Emergency Care:
No emergency care is available at this facility. For after hours assistance, call 1-866-862-7863 or for emergency care call 911.
