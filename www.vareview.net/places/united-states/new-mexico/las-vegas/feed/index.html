Services and Scope of Care:

  • Blood Draws (by appt. only)
  • EKGs (Electrocardiograms)
  • Evaluations by Doctors
  • Routine Lab Tests

Location:
      624 University Ave., Las Vegas, NM 87701

Health Net federal Service customer service phone number is 1-800-621-6499

Eligibility:
For information about enrollment in the Las Vegas CBOC call Eligibility and Enrollment at (505) 265-1711, Ext. 2741.

  • Application for Care can be faxed to:  (505) 256-2808

Co-Pay:  Co-Pay may apply.

Other Pertinent Information:

  • For appointments, call the Clinic at (505) 425-1910.
  • To assist in your care and treatment, please bring all of your medications to your first appointment.

Emergency Care:
      No emergency care is available at this facility. For after hours assistance, call 1-866-862-7863 or for emergency care call 911.
