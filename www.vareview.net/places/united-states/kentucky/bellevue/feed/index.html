  • Primary care services for Veterans in the Bellevue and surrounding areas of Campbell County.
  • Routine Medical Care with highly qualified primary care providers.
  • Podiatry Services.
  • Teleretinal Services.
  • Referrals for other specialty clinics are available to the Parent Facility – Cincinnati VA Medical Center.
  • Nutrition Counseling.
  • Behavioral Health and Social Work services including individual, group and family counseling, and programs such as Smoking Cessation.
  • Laboratory: Blood drawing and EKG services available.
  • Clinical Pharmacist (medicine education).
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or can be picked up at the Parent Facility.
  • Home Based Primary Care services.
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all Veterans.

Driving Directions

From Southbound 471:  Take 471 south across the Ohio River.  Take the first exit (exit 5 Bellevue-Newport).  At the end of the ramp turn right onto Cowen Drive (Route 8).  Go under the expressway overpass and continue to traffic light at the corner of Cowen and Riviera Drive.  (Party Source on the right).  Turn right onto Riviera Drive and look for Frisch’s.  Turn left onto Landmark Drive.  Medical Arts building will be on the left hand side.  The VA clinic is located on the 3rd floor.

From Northbound 471:  Take 471 north to exit 5 (Bellevue-Newport).  At the end of the ramp turn right onto Cowen Drive (Rte 8).  Go to next  traffic light at the corner of Cowen and Riviera Drive (Party Source on right) and turn right onto Riviera Drive and look for Frisch’s.  Turn left onto Landmark Drive.  Medical Arts building will be on the left hand side.  The VA clinic is located on the 3rd floor.

Parking

Free on premises.
