  • Primary care services for veterans in Floyd and surrounding counties.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the Huntington VA Medical Center.
  • Laboratory, optometry, podiatry, women’s health and mental health services.
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet. All medications prescribed are in accordance with VA guidelines.

Driving Directions

The clinic is located between Paintsville and Prestonsburg on Kentucky Route 321. Turn off U.S. 23 at the Route 3 north sign, turn left onto Kentucky Route 321 for approximately .25 miles. Clinic is on the right.

Parking

Ample free parking on premises
