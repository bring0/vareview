Driving Directions

From I 95, take Georgia Hwy 40 exit # 3, east towards Kingsland. Travel 2.2 miles on HWY 40 to Kings Bay Road where you will turn left or north. Then after traveling 0.2 miles turn right or east on Lake Shore Drive.  Make the first right onto Lake Shore Point Road. The St. Marys CBOC is the third building on the left – it is located on the grounds of the Southeast Georgia Health System.
