Conveniently located close to Hunter Army Air Field.

  • Provides primary care, mental health, physical therapy, and specialty care including dermatology, gynecology, ophthalmology and podiatry.
  • Serves approximately 8,000 patients in 10 Georgia and South Carolina counties.

Distance to Charleston VAMC: 84 miles.

Parking

  • Free on premises

Directions

  • Merge onto I-516 East/GA-21 South/WM F Lynes Pkwy. East via Exit 164A
  • I-516 East becomes W. De Renne Ave.
  • Turn RIGHT onto White Bluff Rd.
  • Turn RIGHT onto Abercorn St./GA-204
