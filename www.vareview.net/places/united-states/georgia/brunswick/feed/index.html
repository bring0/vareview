  • Primary care, & mental health, optometry, social work, podiatry and prosthetic, pharmaceutical counseling and dietary services for Veterans in Brunswick, GA area. Other services will be available through the use of tele-health.
  • Handicapped accessible: full and easy access for all Veterans.
  • Personal care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the Carl Vinson VA Medical Center.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.
