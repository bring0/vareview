  • Primary Care services for veterans
  • Behavioral Health services available
  • Handicapped accessible
  • Personal care.  Highly qualified primary care providers.  Specialty care referrals to the Oscar G. Johnson Medical Center in Iron Mountain, Michigan.
  • Laboratory:  Mon. – Thurs. 7:30am – 10:30am; Blood drawing services available.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Driving Directions

  • From the East:  Follow US-8 West (Kemp Street).  The clinic has a large blue awning and is located across from Culvers.
  • From the West:  Follow US-8 East (Kemp Street).  The clinic has a large blue awning and is located across from Culvers.

Parking

  • Free on premises.  Handicapped parking is available in front of the clinic.  Overflow parking is available behind the clinic.
