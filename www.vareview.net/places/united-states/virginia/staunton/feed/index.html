Places in Staunton, Virginia, United States | VA Review http://www.vareview.net Tue, 02 Aug 2016 15:02:43 +0000 en-US hourly 1 Staunton CBOC http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/virginia/staunton/outpatient-clinic/staunton-cboc/ http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/virginia/staunton/outpatient-clinic/staunton-cboc/#reviews Mon, 25 Jul 2016 17:34:19 +0000 http://www.vareview.net/?post_type=gd_place&p=1435 read more]]> The Staunton Community Based Outpatient Clinic (CBOC) is an extension of the Salem VAMC’s Primary Care and Mental Health outpatient clinics. Veterans living in Staunton and the surrounding areas will now be able to receive primary care and mental health services at the new clinic when medically and logistically appropriate.

Driving Directions: (Note:  Address is now 102 Lacy B. King Way; Staunton, VA  24401.)

DIRECTIONS TO STAUNTON CBOC (FROM 81N):
• 81 NORTH (APPROXIMATELY 77 MILES FROM SALEM)
• STAUNTON EXIT 220-ROUTE 262 (STAY ON APPROXIMATELY 3 MILES)
• GO TO 3RD EXIT (1ST EXIT IS ROUTE 11, 2nd EXIT IS OLD GREENVILLE RD)
• EXIT ON MIDDLEBROOK ROAD-ROUTE 252
• AT END OF EXIT RAMP IS A STOP SIGN; MAKE A RIGHT ONTO MIDDLEBROOK ROAD
• THIS IS FOUR LANES THAT WILL TURN INTO TWO LANES; TRAVEL APPROXIMATELY .4 MILES AND TAKE A LEFT INTO “MIDDLEBROOK PARK”, ONTO Lacy B. King WAY. THE CBOC IS STRAIGHT AHEAD, SLIGHTLY TOWARD THE LEFT

DIRECTIONS TO STAUNTON CBOC (FROM DOWNTOWN STAUNTON):

• GO SOUTH ON AUGUSTA STREET, HEADING TOWARD COURTHOUSES. STAY ON AUGUSTA STREET, WHICH WILL BARE TOWARD THE RIGHT AND TURN INTO MIDDLEBROOK AVENUE.
• CONTINUE STAYING ON MIDDLEBROOK AVENUE WHERE YOU’LL GO THROUGH A SERIES OF LIGHTS. STAY IN LEFT LANE (AS THE RIGHT LANE WILL EVENTUALLY NEED TO TURN RIGHT AND YOU NEED TO STAY ON MIDDLEBROOK, HEADING WEST TOWARD 262.
• MIDDLEBROOK AVE WILL BE TWO LANES…YOU NEED TO TURN RIGHT AT BUSINESS WAY (WHICH IS JUST BEFORE MIDDLEBROOK TURNS INTO FOUR LANES).
• GO UP THE HILL, PASSING DODSON PEST CONTROL AND SETH DRIVE ON RIGHT.
• LOOK FOR FLAGPOLE AND BRICK BUILDING WITH SIGN OUT FRONT FOR CBOC.

• TRAVEL APPROXIMATELY .4 MILES AND TAKE A LEFT INTO “MIDDLEBROOK PARK”, ONTO
Lacy B. King WAY. THE CBOC IS STRAIGHT AHEAD, SLIGHTLY TOWARD THE LEFT
