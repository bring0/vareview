*** PLEASE NOTE:  MAILING ADDRESS is PO Box 645; Tazewell, VA  24651 ***

Features

The CBOC is handicapped accessible, and functions as a primary care clinic. Services that are provided include:

  • Referrals for specialty services through the Salem VA Medical Center.
  • Routine laboratory and x-rays as requested by the provider at the clinic.
  • Medications as prescribed by the provider at the clinic, and processed through the mail.
  • Preventative health screenings.

Driving Directions

  • From I-581, merge onto I-77 N via Exit 72 toward Bluefield/Charleston, WV.
  • Go 17.5 miles to Exit 58 toward US-52/Bastian.
  • Turn left onto VA-666.
  • Turn left onto US-52.
  • Turn right onto VA-614.
  • Go 13.5 miles and take slight left onto VA-61.
  • Turn left onto Ben Bolt Ave.
  • Clinic is located at 123 Ben Bolt Ave.

Parking

  • Parking is free
