  • The CBOC is handicapped accessible.
  • Functions as a Primary Care clinic.
  • Referrals for specialty services through the Salem VAMC.
  • Routine laboratory and x-rays as requested by the provider at the clinic.
  • Medications as prescribed by the provider at the clinic, and processed through the mail.
  • Preventive health screenings.
  • Mental Health services are available and include individual, group, and family counseling, and programs such as Tobacco Cessation, Substance Abuse, Depression/Pain Management, and Women’s Trauma Group.

Driving Directions

From Route 220S:

  • US-220 becomes US-58 East. Take the US-58 Business ramp toward Martinsville.
  • Turn right onto US-58 Business Route East.
  • Take immediate slight left onto US-58 East.
  • Proceed 14.3 miles.
  • US-58 East becomes US-58 Business Route East.
  • Proceed 7.8 miles.
  • Take the US-29 Business Route North / VA-86 North ramp toward Lynchburg.
  • Merge onto US-29 Business Route North.
  • Proceed 1.6 miles to 705 Piney Forest Road, next to Save-a-Lot Grocery; behind Papa John’s Pizza in the Piney Forest Shopping Center.

From South Boston:

  • From US-501 Business Route South / Main Street, follow US-501 Business Route South 0.7 miles.
  • Turn right onto US-360 West / US-58 West.
  • Continue to follow US-360 West 29.7 miles.
  • US-360 West becomes Riverside Dr. / US-58 Business Route West. Proceed 1.1 miles.
  • Turn right onto Audubon Dr. Proceed 0.9 miles.
  • Turn right onto US-29 Business Route / Piney Forest Rd.
  • Proceed 0.2 miles to 705 Piney Forest Road, located next to Save-a-Lot Grocery; behind Papa John’s Pizza in the Piney Forest Shopping Center.

From Chatham:

  • Start out going southeast on US-29 Business Route / Main Street toward Pitt Street for 0.2 miles.
  • Turn left onto Halifax Rd. and proceed 0.8 miles.
  • Merge onto US-29 south toward Danville.
  • Proceed 8.8 miles.
  • Take slight right onto US-29 Business Route South.
  • Proceed 7.1 miles to 705 Piney Forest Road, located next to Save-a-Lot Grocery; behind Papa John’s Pizza in the Piney Forest Shopping Center.

From Eden:

  • From NC-700 East / NC-770 East / West Meadow Rd., follow NC-770 East (crossing into Virginia) and proceed 8 miles.
  • NC-770 East becomes Berry Hill Rd.
  • Proceed 7.5 miles.
  • Turn Right onto US-58 Business Route East.
  • Proceed 7 miles.
  • Take the US-29-Business Route North / VA-86 North ramp toward Lynchburg.
  • Proceed 0.1 miles.
  • Merge onto US-29 Business Route North.
  • Proceed 1.6 miles to 705 Piney Forest Road, located next to Save-a-Lot Grocery; behind Papa John’s Pizza in the Piney Forest Shopping Center.
