  • The CBOC is handicapped accessible.
  • Functions as a Primary Care clinic.
  • Referrals for specialty services through the Salem VAMC.
  • Routine laboratory and x-rays as requested by the provider at the clinic.
  • Medications as prescribed by the provider at the clinic, and processed through the mail.
  • Preventive health screenings.
  • Mental Health services are available and include individual, group, and family counseling, and programs such as Tobacco Cessation, Substance Abuse, and Depression/Pain Management.
