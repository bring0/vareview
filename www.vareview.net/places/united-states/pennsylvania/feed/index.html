If you are a new patient, you may call 1 -800-409-8771, extension 6000, for information on scheduling appointments in these clinics. If you are already receiving VA care, you may call directly to your assigned clinic for information on appointments or other questions.

DIRECTIONS:

From Hanover: Take Route 30 East to Memory Lane. Go right at the end of the ramp onto Memory Lane. Turn right on Eastern Blvd. Turn right on South Royal St. Turn left into the parking lot. Our clinic is on the first floor to your right as you enter the building.

From Lancaster: Take Route 30 West to Mt Zion Rd (Rt. 24). Go left at the end of the ramp onto Mt. Zion Rd. Turn right on Eastern Blvd. Turn right on South Royal St. Turn left into the parking lot. Our clinic is on the first floor to your right as you enter the building.

From Lebanon: Take Route 72 South, to Route 30 West, to Mt Zion Road (Rt. 24). Go left at the end of the ramp onto Mt. Zion Rd. Turn right on Eastern Blvd. Turn right on South Royal St. Turn left into the parking lot. Our clinic is on the first floor to your right as you enter the building.

From Harrisburg: Take 83 South. Get off at Exit 21–Route 30 East. Take Route 30 East to Memory Lane. Go right at the end of the ramp. Turn right on Eastern Blvd. Turn right on South Royal St. Turn left into the parking lot. Our clinic is on the first floor to your right as you enter the building.

From Stewartstown/Shrewsbury: Take 83 North. Get off at Exit 19. Go right at the end of the ramp onto East Market Street (Route 462). Turn right on South Royal St. Turn right into the parking lot. Our clinic is on the first floor to your right as you enter the building.

SERVICES OFFERED AT THE YORK VA CLINIC:

• Primary Medical Care – male and female
• Women’s Health Care including annual breast and pelvic exams
• Endocrinology
• Optometry with retinal imaging
• Physical Therapy
• Occupational Therapy (counseling and evaluation)
• Home Oxygen Therapy
• Neurology (follow up evaluation via telehealth)
• Dermatology (telehealth)
Telehealth:
As a convenience to our patients, some services are offered by electronic means. Individual or group appointments may be performed via video to the Lebanon VAMC or other medical facility. Also, various tests can now be performed at any one of the six outpatient clinics, with the analysis being performed at the Lebanon VAMC or other medical facility.

Educational Services:

• Smoking Cessation
• MOVE! (weight loss)
• Coping Skills
• Anger Management*
• Nutrition

Laboratory Services:

• Laboratory testing
• Finger stick Coumadin testing

Support Services:

• Social work services
• Pharmacy Counseling
• MyHealtheVet authentication

Behavioral Health Services – group and/or individual therapy:

• Psychiatry
• Psychology
• PTSD (post traumatic stress disorder) – evaluation and treatment
• Substance abuse evaluation and treatment
• Geriatrics

*denotes service may not be available at this time…coming soon though!

Talented physicians, physician assistants, nurse practitioners, nurses, pharmacists, psychologists and social workers deliver high quality VA health care through Patient Aligned Care Teams (PACT).  PACT is a patient-centered care model with an emphasis on primary and mental health care integration, and health promotion and disease prevention.
