  • Medication management
  • Diabetes management
  • Dietitian Services
  • Patient-Family health education
  • Patient assessment
  • Care planning
  • Nursing services
  • Phlebotomy
  • Telephone care

Behavioral Health services are available on-site at the staffed CBOCs and are available by fee basis from the contracted CBOC. Services available include intake, all varieties of counseling, and medication management. Services are available either ‘live’ or via video teleconferencing.

Any veteran wishing to receive services at one of our CBOCs can telephone the CBOC directly or contact the Erie VA at 1-800-274-8387.
