In an attempt to better serve our Veterans in the areas in which they live, we have established community-based outpatient clinics in Berks, Dauphin, Lancaster, Schuylkill, and York Counties in addition to the primary care clinics located on our main campus in Lebanon. If you are a new patient, you may call 1 -800-409-8771, extension 6000, for information on scheduling appointments in these clinics. If you are already receiving VA care, you may call directly to your assigned clinic for information on appointments or other questions.

Camp Hill VA Clinic
25 N 32nd Street
Camp Hill, PA 17011
717-730-9782
Hours: Monday-Friday 7:30am-4pm

DIRECTIONS:

From the Shippensburg /Chambersburg/Carlisle Area: Take 81 North to 581 East (Camp Hill exit). Exit off the Camp Hill Exit ( Rte 15 N) onto 32nd St in Camp Hill. Go through 3 traffic lights and just through the 3rd traffic light on the right is the Camp Hill VA Clinic.

From the Hanover/Gettysburg/Dillsburg Area: Take Route 15 North to Camp Hill. After crossing the overpass, go through 3 traffic lights and just through the 3rd traffic light on the right is the Camp Hill VA Clinic.

From the York Area: Take 83 North to the Camp Hill/Harrisburg split and follow the signs for Camp Hill. Exit onto 581 West. Exit 581 West at the Camp Hill Exit (Route 15 N). Go through 3 traffic lights and the clinic will be on your right just through the 3rd traffic light.

SERVICES OFFERED AT THE CAMP HILL VA CLINIC:

• Primary Medical Care – male and female
• Women’s Health Care including annual breast and pelvic exams
• Endocrinology
• Renal
• Podiatry
• Optometry
• Physical Therapy
• Occupational Therapy
• Home Oxygen Therapy*
• Neurology (follow up evaluation via telehealth)
• Dermatology (telehealth)

Telehealth:
As a convenience to our patients, some services are offered by electronic means. Individual or group appointments may be performed via video to the Lebanon VAMC or other medical facility. Also, various tests can now be performed at any one of the six outpatient clinics, with the analysis being performed at the Lebanon VAMC or other medical facility.

Educational Services:

• Diabetes Education
• Cardiac Risk Reduction
• Lifestyle changes (diabetes, cholesterol, blood pressure)
• MOVE! (weight loss)
• Nutrition
• Anger Management*

Support Services

• Social work services
• Female veterans support
• Vietnam veterans support
• Pharmacy Counseling*
• MyHealtheVet authentication

Laboratory Services:
• Laboratory testing
• Finger stick Coumadin testing*
• Immunizations

Behavioral Health Services – group and/or individual therapy:

• Psychiatry
• Psychology
• Substance Abuse
• PTSD (post traumatic stress disorder)
• Geriatrics
• Memory disorders

*denotes service may not be available at this time…coming soon though!
