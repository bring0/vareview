Saracini Community Based Outpatient Clinic http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/pennsylvania/horsham/outpatient-clinic/victor-j-saracini-community-based-outpatient-clinic/ http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/pennsylvania/horsham/outpatient-clinic/victor-j-saracini-community-based-outpatient-clinic/#reviews Wed, 27 Jul 2016 01:47:55 +0000 http://www.vareview.net/?post_type=gd_place&p=1606 read more]]> The Community Based Outpatient Clinic located in Horsham, PA provides primary care services for Veterans in Montogomery County, PA and surrounding areas by highly qualified primary care providers, with specialty care referrals to the Philadelphia VA Medical Center.

It also provides behavioral health services including individual, group and family counseling, and programs such as QuitSmart Smoking Cessation.

Speciality Clinics operating at this location include Podiatry and Social Work, as well as blood drawing services.

The clinic is handicapped accessible providing full and easy access for all Veterans.

Telehealth Services
Some services (listed below) at the Victor J. Saricini CBOC are provided through remote connection to the Philadelphia VA Medical Center. If you are in need of these services, please speak with your provider or other clinic staff for further information.

  • PADRECC (Parkinson’s Disease)
  • MOVE! Nutrition
  • Insomnia
  • Sleep Apnea
  • Diabetic Retinal Screening
  • Dermatology Imaging
  • Social Work
  • Diabetes Nutrition
  • Chronic Pain
  • Behavioral Health Support for Returning Veterans
  • PTSD – Prolonged Exposure (coming soon)
  • Genetic Counseling (coming soon)
