If you are a new patient, you may call 1 -800-409-8771, extension 6000, for information on scheduling appointments in these clinics. If you are already receiving VA care, you may call directly to your assigned clinic for information on appointments or other questions.

DIRECTIONS:
From Reading: Follow PA-61 North into Frackville.  Turn right onto Spruce Street.  Clinic is located at 10 East Spruce Street.

From Hazleton: Take Interstate 81 south to the Frackville exit 124B (PA-61 North). Turn right onto Spruce Street.  Clinic is located at 10 East Spruce Street.

From Harrisburg: Take Interstate 81 north to the Frackville exit (PA-61 North).  Turn right onto Spruce street.  Clinic is located at 10 East Spruce Street.

From Lebanon: Take Route 72 north to Interstate 81.  Take Interstate 81 North.  Take the Frackville exit (PA-61 North).  Turn right onto Spruce Street.  Clinic is located at 10 East Spruce Street.
