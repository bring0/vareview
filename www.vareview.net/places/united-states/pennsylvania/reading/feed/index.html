If you are a new patient, you may call 1 -800-409-8771, extension 6000, for information on scheduling appointments in these clinics. If you are already receiving VA care, you may call directly to your assigned clinic for information on appointments or other questions.

DIRECTIONS:

FROM THE WEST:  Travel Route 422 East.  Turn left at Mull Road (McDonald’s and Dunkin Donuts on right).  Mull Avenue becomes Van Reed Road.  Cross over State Hill Road.  Continue and turn right onto Papermill Road (Turkey Hill on left).  Continue to second red light and make right onto Century Boulevard.  Clinic is up hill on right side.

FROM THE EAST:  Travel Route 222 West.  Take the Spring Ridge Drive exit.  Turn left onto Papermill Road.  Continue to red light and make left onto Century Boulevard.  Clinic is up hill on right side.

SERVICES OFFERED AT READING VA CLINIC:
• Primary Medical Care – male and female
• Women’s Health Care including  annual breast and pelvic exams
• Occupational therapy (counseling and evaluation)
• Neurology (follow up evaluation via telehealth)
• Dermatology (telehealth)
• Retinal imaging

Telehealth:
As a convenience to our patients, some services are offered by electronic means.  Individual or group appointments may be performed via video to the Lebanon VAMC or other medical facility.  Also, various tests can now be performed at any one of the six outpatient clinics, with the analysis being performed at the Lebanon VAMC or other medical facility.

Educational Services:

• Diabetes Education
• Smoking Cessation
• Coping Skills
• Operation Enduring Freedom/Operation Iraqi Freedom/Operation New Dawn (OEF/OIF/OND)
• Stress Management
• Nutrition

Support Services:

• Social work services
• Pharmacy Counseling*
• MyHealtheVet authentication

Laboratory Services:

• Laboratory testing
• Finger stick Coumadin testing*

Behavioral Health Services – group and/or individual therapy:

• Psychiatry
• Psychology
• PTSD (post traumatic stress disorder) – evaluation and treatment

*denotes service may not be available at the present time…coming soon though!
