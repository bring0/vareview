If you are a new patient, you may call 1 -800-409-8771, extension 6000, for information on scheduling appointments in these clinics. If you are already receiving VA care, you may call directly to your assigned clinic for information on appointments or other questions.

Lancaster VA Clinic
Green Field Corporate Center
1861 Charter Lane, Suite 120
Lancaster, PA 17601
717-290-6900
Hours: Monday-Friday 7:30am-4pm

DIRECTIONS:

From Lebanon: Take Rte 72 South to Rte 283 East. Merge onto Rte 30 East. Exit onto Greenfield Road going right on the exit ramp onto the bridge. At the third traffic light, turn right onto William Penn Way. Take your first right onto, Charter Lane. Turn right into the 3rd driveway and we are at Suite 120.

From Reading or Denver area: Take Rte 222 South to Rt 30 East. Exit onto Greenfield Road going right on the exit ramp onto the bridge. At the third traffic light, turn right onto William Penn Way. Take your first right onto Charter Lane. Turn right into the third driveway and we are Suite 120.

From Gap, Strasburg area: Take 30 West passing Rockvale Square, exit right onto Greenfield Road. Turn right at the light. At the second light, turn right onto William Penn Way. Take your first right onto Charter Lane. Turn right into the third driveway and we are Suite 120.

SERVICES OFFERED AT THE LANCASTER VA CLINIC:

• Primary Medical Care – male and female
• Women’s Health Care including annual breast and pelvic exams
• Podiatry
• Optometry with retinal imaging
• Occupational Therapy (counseling and evaluation)
• Neurology (follow up evaluation via telehealth)
• Dermatology (telehealth)

Telehealth:
As a convenience to our patients, some services are offered by electronic means. Individual or group appointments may be performed via video to the Lebanon VAMC or other medical facility. Also, various tests can now be performed at any one of the six outpatient clinics, with the analysis being performed at the Lebanon VAMC or other medical facility.

Educational Services:

• Cardiac Risk Reduction
• Coping Skills

Laboratory Services:

• Laboratory testing
• Finger stick Coumadin testing*

Support Services:

• Social work services
• Pharmacy Counseling*
• MyHealtheVet authentication

Behavioral Health Services – group and/or individual therapy:

• Psychiatry
• Psychology
• PTSD (post traumatic stress disorder) – evaluation and treatment
• Geriatrics evaluation

*denotes service may not be available at the present time…coming soon though!
