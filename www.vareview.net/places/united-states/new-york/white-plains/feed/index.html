Are you a Veteran in need of women’s health care?  We offer VA women’s health services in one convenient location in White Plains.  Health care services include gynecology, pre-conception counseling, reproductive health, mental health, social work and telehealth.

Visit the Women’s Health Specialist at the VA White Plains Center which is conveniently located across from the Westchester Mall on S Broadway.  Parking is adjacent to the center in the municipal parking lot on Martin Street.
