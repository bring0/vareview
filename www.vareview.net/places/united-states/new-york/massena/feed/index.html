Services:

  • Preventive Care
  • Chronic Disease Management
  • Vaccination/Immunizations
  • Patient Education
  • Weight Reduction Education
  • MOVE Program
  • Tobacco Cessation
  • Mental Health Programs
  • PTSD Counseling
  • TBI Evaluation
  • Military Sexual Trauma
  • My HealtheVet
  • EKG Tests
  • Women’s Health
  • Medication Counseling
  • Anti Coagulation Clinic
  • Onsite phlebotomy services
  • Group and individual counseling

 

Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.

Personal Care: A health care team provides primary care services at the clinic, and subcontract routine radiology procedures. Patients with specialized medical needs are treated at VA Health Care Upstate New York at Syracuse.

Laboratory: Blood drawing and analysis services available.

Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail. Emergency medicines provided.

Radiology: Chest, abdominal, and extremity x-rays available through a subcontract.

Parking: Free parking is available.

 

We offer the following Telehealth services. Please ask your primary care provider for more information.

  • Anticoagulation
  • Audiology
  • Cardiology
  • Congestive Heart Failure (CHF)
  • Diabetes Education
  • Genomics
  • GI
  • GU
  • Neurology
  • Nutrition
  • Orthopedics
  • Pain
  • Rheumatology
  • Surgery
  • Skin Evaluation
  • TeleDerm
  • TeleRetinal
  • Mental Health (Psychiatry, Substance Abuse, Therapy and PTSD)
  •  Womens Health
