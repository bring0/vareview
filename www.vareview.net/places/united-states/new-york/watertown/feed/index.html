  • Primary care services for Veterans in the greater Watertown New York area.
  • Behavioral Health Care: a behavioral health care team consisting of social workers, psychiatrists, a psychiatric nurse, and visiting counselors from the Syracuse VA, provide medications, individual and group psychotherapy for Veterans experiencing personal and emotional problems, including drug and alcohol problems.
  • Smoking Cessation: the clinic offers the QuitSmart® smoking cessation program — that combines the best of behavioral and medication treatments for smoking cessation in a group format.
  • Specialty services are available through a referral to the Syracuse VA Medical Center by your VA primary care provider.Laboratory: blood drawing and analysis services available.
  • Prescriptions: routine prescriptions are available.
  • Radiology: chest, abdominal, and extremity x-rays available.

Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.

Some transportation services are available through DAV. For more information please call 315-425-4352.

 

Parking: Free parking on premises

We also offer Home Telehealth services. Please ask your primary care provider for more information.
