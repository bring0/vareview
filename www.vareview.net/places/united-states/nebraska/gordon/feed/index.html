  • Primary Care services for veterans in the Gordon area.
  • Handicapped accessible for full and easy access to all veterans.
  • Personal Care:  Highly qualified Primary Care providers, specialty care referrals to the VA Black Hills Health Care System, Fort Meade and Hot Springs Campuses.
  • Prescriptions are processed by mail or My HealtheVet.

Driving Directions:
Turn north off Highway 20 to Main Street byway 27.  Continue north to East 8th Street and turn right (east) on East 8th Street.  Enter the building by the frontdoor, and up a few stairs to the first floor.  Handicapped elevator is to the right.  Turn right down the hallway to elevator on the left.  Take elevator to basement, turn right again down the basement hallway.  Waiting room will be on the right.
