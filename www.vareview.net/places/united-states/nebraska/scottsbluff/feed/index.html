Parent Facility
VA Black Hills Health Care System

Features:

  • Primary Care services for veterans in the Scottsbluff area.
  • Handicapped accessible for full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Personal Care:  Highly qualified Primary Care providers, specialty care referrals to the VA Black Hills Health Care System, Fort Meade and Hot Springs Campuses.
  • Prescriptions are processed by mail or My HealtheVet.

Driving Directions:
Take Highway 385 South (crossing into Nebraska) for 127 miles, slight right on to NE-62A LINK.  NE-62A LINK becomes US-26 West, turn left on to 21st Avenue; turn right on to E 17th Street and then right on to E. Portal Place.

Parking is free on the premises.
