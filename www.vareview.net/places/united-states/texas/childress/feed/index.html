The Childress VA Community Based Outpatient Clinic (CBOC) is designed to deliver primary care services to eligible Veterans living in Childress County and outlying areas.

Services and Scope of Care:

  • Health screening
  • Health promotion
  • Health maintenance
  • Education
  • Management of acute and chronic diseases
