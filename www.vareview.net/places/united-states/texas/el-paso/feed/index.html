The El Paso Eastside Community Based Outpatient Clinic (CBOC) is designed to deliver primary care services to eligible veterans.

Services and Scope of Care:

  • Health promotion
  • Health Screening
  • Health maintenance
  • Education
  • Management of acute and chronic diseases
  • Routine care, such as:
    * Primary Care
    * Immunizations
    * Mental Health
  • Telephone: (915) 217-2428 to set up an appointment or for information about the East El Paso Clinic.

Hours of Operation:

  • Monday through Friday 8:00 am – 4:45 pm

Location:

  • 2400 Trawood Drive Suite 200
    El Paso, Texas 79936

Eligibility:

  • For information about enrollment at the El Paso Eastside Community Based Clinic call at (915) 217-2428.

Co-pay:

  • Co-Pay may apply

Other Pertinent Information:

  • No walk-in appointments are available at this facility, but some same day appointments may be available with assigned providers. For appointments, call (915) 217-2428.
  • To assist in your care and treatment, please bring a list of all prescribed medications to your first appointment.

Emergency Care:

  • No emergency care is available at this facility. For after hours emergency care call 911.
