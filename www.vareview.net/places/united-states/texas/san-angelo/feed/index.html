Places in San Angelo, Texas, United States | VA Review http://www.vareview.net Tue, 02 Aug 2016 15:02:43 +0000 en-US hourly 1 San Angelo Clinic http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/texas/san-angelo/outpatient-clinic/san-angelo-clinic/ http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/texas/san-angelo/outpatient-clinic/san-angelo-clinic/#reviews Tue, 19 Jul 2016 15:51:46 +0000 http://www.vareview.net/?post_type=gd_place&p=886 read more]]> The San Angelo Community Based Outpatient Clinic (CBOC) is designed to deliver primary care and Mental Health services to eligible veterans living in the following counties: Coke, Glasscock, Irion, Reagan, Sterling and Tom Green.

Services and Scope of Care:

  • Health screening
  • Health promotion
  • Health maintenance
  • Education
  • Management of acute and chronic diseases
  • Mental Health including Substance Use
  • Social Work
  • Women’s Health
  • Telehealth
  • Home Based Primary Care

Eligibility:

  • For information about enrollment in the San Angelo Community Based Clinic call Eligibility and Enrollment at 432-263-7361, ext. 7347 or 1-800-472-1365, Ext. 7347.

Co-Pay:

  • Co-Pay may apply.

Other Pertinent Information:

  • For appointments, call your PACT Team, (325) 658-6138
  • Team 1 Ext. 2460
  • Team 2 Ext. 2497
  • To assist in your care and treatment, please bring all of your medications to your first appointment.

Emergency Care:

  • No emergency care is available at this facility.  For emergency care call 911.
