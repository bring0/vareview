Bell Blvd. which is only about a mile away from the current clinic location.  (see map below)

Features

  • Primary care services for veterans in Cedar Park, TX area.
  • Behavioral Health services including individual, group and family counseling, and programs such as QuitSmart Smoking Cessation.
  • Women’s Health Services
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the Central Texas Veterans Health Care System.
  • Laboratory: Blood drawing services available from 7:00am to 2:00pm.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.
  • Benefit and Claim Counselor available for assistance

Driving Directions

  • From IH35 – take FM 1431 and head west about eight (8) miles. FM 1431 is “Whitestone Blvd.” once you enter Cedar Park. The clinic is on your right.
  • From Highway 183 – from the South, turn right, or from the North, turn left, onto FM 1431 (Whitestone Blvd.) and the clinic is on your left.
  • From the 183A Toll Road, exit for FM 1431, head west, and the clinic is on the right.

Parking

  • Free on premises
