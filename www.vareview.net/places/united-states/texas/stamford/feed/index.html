Services and Scope of Care:

  • Health screening
  • Health promotion
  • Health maintenance
  • Education
  • Management of acute and chronic diseases
  • Women’s Health

Hours of Operation:

  • Call for appointment

Eligibility:

  • For information about enrollment in the Stamford Community Based Clinic call Eligibility and Enrollment at 1-800-472-1365 Ext. 7347.

    Co-Pay:  Co-Pay may apply.

Other Pertinent Information:

  • For appointments, call the West Texas VA Health Care System (432) 263-7361, ext. 5692 or 1-800-472-1365 ext. 5692.
  • To assist in your care and treatment, please bring all of your medications to your first appointment.

Emergency Care:

  • No emergency care is available at this facility.  For emergency care call 911.
