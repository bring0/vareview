Places in Midland, Texas, United States | VA Review http://www.vareview.net Tue, 02 Aug 2016 15:02:43 +0000 en-US hourly 1 Permian Basin Community Based Outpatient Clinic http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/texas/midland/outpatient-clinic/permian-basin-community-based-outpatient-clinic/ http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/texas/midland/outpatient-clinic/permian-basin-community-based-outpatient-clinic/#reviews Tue, 19 Jul 2016 15:48:18 +0000 http://www.vareview.net/?post_type=gd_place&p=883 read more]]> The Permian Basin Community Based Clinic is designed to bring primary care services for eligible veterans living in the following counties:  Loving, Winkler, Ector, Midland, Ward, Crane and Upton.

Services and Scope of Care:

  • Health screening
  • Health promotion
  • Health maintenance
  • Education
  • Management of acute & chronic diseases
  • Mental Health, including Psychology & Substance Use
  • Women’s Health
  • Audiology
  • Telehealth
  • Optometry
  • Home Based Primary Care
  • Physical Therapy

Eligibility:

  • For information about enrollment in the Permian Basin Community Based Clinic call Eligibility and Enrollment at 1-800-472-1365 Ext. 7347 (service area only).

Co-Pay:

  • Co-Pay may apply.

Other Pertinent Information:

  • For appointments, call your PACT Team (432)685-2110
  • Team 1 Ext. 2351
  • Team 2 Ext. 2353
  • Team 3 Ext. 2355
  • To assist in your care and treatment, please bring all of your medications to your first appointment.

Emergency Care:

  • No emergency care is available at this facility.  For emergency care call 911.
