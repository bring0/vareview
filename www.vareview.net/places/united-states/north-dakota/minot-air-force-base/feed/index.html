  • Primary care services for veterans in the Minot area.
  • Behavioral Health services.
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the Fargo VA Healthcare System.
  • Laboratory: Blood drawing services available.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail, My HealtheVet, or telephone.

Driving Directions

From Minot take Hwy 83 North.  Turn left onto Missile Avenue.  End at 10 Missile Ave.  (Note: The Minot CBOC is located on the Minot Air Force Base.  Patients will need to go through base security.)

Parking

  • Free on premises.
