  • Primary care services for veterans in the Jamestown area.
  • Behavioral Health services.
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the Fargo VA Medical Center.
  • Laboratory: Blood drawing services available.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail,  My HealtheVet, or by telephone.

Driving Directions

From I-94 take exit 256 and turn left onto US-281-Truck N and head south .7 miles.  Turn left onto 20th Street SW.  The Jamestown VA Outpatient Clinic is located within the Jamestown Regional Medical Center.

Parking

  • Free on premises.
