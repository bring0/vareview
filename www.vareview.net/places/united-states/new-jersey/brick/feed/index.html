Howard (Brick, NJ) Community-Based Outpatient Clinic http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/new-jersey/brick/outpatient-clinic/james-j-howard-brick-nj-community-based-outpatient-clinic/ http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/new-jersey/brick/outpatient-clinic/james-j-howard-brick-nj-community-based-outpatient-clinic/#reviews Thu, 28 Jul 2016 01:39:28 +0000 http://www.vareview.net/?post_type=gd_place&p=1804 read more]]> Features
  • Primary care services for veterans in the New Jersey and surrounding areas.
  • Behavioral Health services including individual, group and family counseling.
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the East Orange and Lyons Campuses.
  • Laboratory: Blood drawing services available.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Standard Services

All appointments are scheduled prior to visit.

  • Primary Care
  • Nutrition
  • Social Work
  • Mental Health

Specialty Clinics

Clinics meet on specific days and appointments are scheduled prior to visit.

  • Allergy
  • Audiology
  • Coumadin
  • Dental
  • Diabetic Consult & Group
  • ENT
  • Physical Therapy
  • Podiatry
  • Spinal Cord Injury
  • Surgery
  • Teleretinal (Diabetic Eye Screening)
  • Urology
  • Women’s Health

Driving Directions

From Garden State Parkway – SOUTHBOUND

-Take Exit 91 and bear left after the toll booth.
-Proceed around the jughandle past the Texaco station.
-Turn right at the light and go back over the parkway.
-Make the 1st right turn at the Department of Veterans Affairs sign. This will be Burrsville Road.
-Proceed approximately 1 mile to the 1st traffic light and turn left onto Jack Martin Blvd.
-Go approximately 1 mile to the next traffic light and turn right onto Rt. 70 West.
-Continue on Rt. 70 West for approximately 1/4 mile and turn right at the Department of Veterans Affairs sign.

From Garden State Parkway- NORTHBOUND

-Take Exit 90 make a right upon exiting and continue for approximately 1 and 1/2 miles.
-Proceed to the 3rd traffic light and turn left onto Route 70 East.
-Continue straight on Rt. 70 to Jack Martin Blvd.
-Go through intersection bearing right around the jughandle and make a left turn at end of jughandle onto Route 70 West.
-Continue on Rt. 70 past Georges Road and turn right at the Department of Veterans Affairs sign

Parking

  • Free on premises
