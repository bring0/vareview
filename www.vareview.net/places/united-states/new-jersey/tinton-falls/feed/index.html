  • Primary care services for veterans in the New Jersey and surrounding areas.
  • Behavioral Health services including individual, group and family counseling.
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the East Orange and Lyons Campuses.
  • Laboratory: Blood drawing services available.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Standard Services

All appointments are scheduled prior to visit.

  • Primary Care
  • Nutrition
  • Social Work
  • Mental Health

Driving Directions

From Middletown/Red Bank:

• Traveling south, take Route 35 over the Navesink Bridge
• At the light (three split), veer right onto Shrewsbury Ave/CR-13
• After crossing Newman Springs Road, travel approximately ½ mile and turn right onto North Gilbert Street
• Follow North Gilbert for one block, the clinic is in The Atrium at 55 N. Gilbert Street, Building #4 (rear of the complex)

From West:

• Take CR-537 (heading east) and then south
• Turn left onto (heading east) onto Sycamore Avenue
• Turn left (north) onto Shrewsbury Avenue/CR-13
• At the next light (2 blocks), turn left (east) onto North Gilbert Street
• Follow North Gilbert for one block, the clinic is in The Atrium at 55 N. Gilbert Street, Building #4 (rear of the complex)

From North:

• Take the Garden State Parkway South to Exit 109 (Red Bank)
• Proceed east on CR 520/Newman Springs
• Turn right (south) onto Shrewsbury Avenue/CR-13, travel approximately ½ mile and turn right onto North Gilbert Street
• Follow North Gilbert for one block, the clinic is in The Atrium at 55 N. Gilbert Street, Building #4 (rear of the complex)

From South:
