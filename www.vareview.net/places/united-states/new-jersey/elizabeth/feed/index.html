  • Primary care services for veterans in the New Jersey and surrounding areas.
  • Behavioral Health services including individual, group and family counseling.
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the East Orange and Lyons Campuses.
  • Laboratory: Blood drawing services available.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Standard Services

All appointments are scheduled prior to visit.

  • Primary Care
  • Nutrition
  • Social Work
  • Mental Health

Driving Directions

From Garden State Parkway:

-Take Exit 137 (Cranford, Roselle Park)
-Turn left at traffic light onto Westfield Ave., and stay on Westfield Ave. for approximately 2 1/2 miles.
-Turn right at Elmora Ave. (White Castle)
-Turn left at 3rd traffic light onto West Jersey St. and continue straight on West Jersey St. crossing Rt. 1 & Rt. 9
-Continue 5 blocks on East Jersey St.
-The VA Elizabeth Clinic is located on the right directly across from the Elizabeth General Medical Clinic -East.

From the New Jersey Turnpike:

-Take Exit 13A, follow signs for Rts. 1 & 9 South to Elizabeth.
-Continue below using directions, ‘From the North’.

From the North:

-Take Rts. 1 & 9 South to Elizabeth.
-Follow (blue H) Hospital signs making a right turn onto Boudinot Place.
-Turn left at stop sign onto Catherine St.
-Turn left at traffic light onto East Jersey St.
-Cross Rts. 1 & 9 and continue 5 blocks on East Jersey St.
-The VA Elizabeth Clinic is located on the right, directly across from the Elizabeth General Medical Center-East.

From the South:

-Take Rts. 1 & 9 North to Elizabeth.
-After the overpass, make a right turn at the traffic light onto East Jersey St.
-The VA Elizabeth Clinic is located on the right, directly across from the Elizabeth General Medical Center-East.

From the West:

-Take Morris Ave. (Rt. 82 East)
-Turn right at North Ave. (North Ave. will become Elmora Ave.)
-Turn left at the 6th traffic light onto West Jersey St. and continue straight on West Jersey St. crossing Rts. 1 & 9.
-Continue 5 blocks on East Jersey St.
-The VA Elizabeth Clinic is located on the right, directly across from the Elizabeth General Medical Center-East.

Parking

  • Free on premises
