  • Primary care services for veterans in the New Jersey and surrounding areas.
  • Behavioral Health services including individual, group and family counseling.
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the East Orange and Lyons Campuses.
  • Laboratory: Blood drawing services available.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Standard Services

All appointments are scheduled prior to visit.

  • Primary Care
  • Nutrition
  • Social Work
  • Mental Health

Specialty Clinics

Clinics meet on specific days and appointments are scheduled prior to visit.

  • Teleretinal (Diabetic Eye Screening)
  • Women’s Health

Driving Directions

From Garden State Parkway – NORTHBOUND & SOUTHBOUND:

-Take the Garden State Parkway to Exit 160, Hackensack.
-At the off-ramp, turn onto Passaic St. (towards Hackensack)
-Travel 2 1/2 miles, passing through Maywood.
-Make a right turn onto Prospect St.
-385 Prospect is located up the 1st ramp to your right.

From Rt. 17 North:

-Exit at Passaic Street.
-Make a right turn from the off-ramp onto Passaic St. (towards Maywood)
-Travel 1 1/2 miles, passing through Maywood.
-Make a right onto Prospect St.

From the East or West:

-Take Rt. 4 to Forest Ave. Exit
-Follow signs towards Maywood which will lead you onto Maywood Ave. (directly off exit ramp)
-Proceed onto Maywood Ave. 0.7 miles, make a left on Passaic St.
-Go approximately 1/2 mile, make a right on Prospect St.
-After turning onto Prospect, 385 Prospect St. is located up the 1st ramp to your right.

Parking

  • Free on premises
