  • Primary care services for veterans in the New Jersey and surrounding areas.
  • Behavioral Health services including individual, group and family counseling.
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the East Orange and Lyons Campuses.
  • Laboratory: Blood drawing services available.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Standard Services

All appointments are scheduled prior to visit.

  • Primary Care
  • Nutrition
  • Social Work
  • Mental Health

Driving Directions

From Newark Airport Area:

-Take I-78 West to Route 24 West
-At the end of Route 24, take I-287 South to Exit 36 (Ridgedale Ave)
-Turn right at light onto Ridgedale Avenue.
-At second light make left onto East Hanover Avenue, at Route 202 in Morris Plains.
-Follow West Hanover Avenue at top of hill will be County facilities on both sides. -The Clinic is in the County Building left-hand side, on the ground floor.

From Northern NJ:

-Take I-80 to I-287 South to Exit 36 (Ridgedale Avenue)
-Turn right at light onto Ridgedale Avenue.
-At second light make left onto East Hanover Avenue (County Road 650). This becomes West Hanover Avenue at Route 202 in Morris Plains.
-Follow West Hanover Avenue; at top of hill will be County facilities on both sides.
-The Clinic is in the County Building left-hand side, on the ground floor.

From Trenton or Southern NJ:

-Take I-287 North to Exit 36A (Morris Avenue [a one way]) and keep to the middle lane,   which becomes Whippany Road (County Road 511).
-Turn left onto East Hanover Avenue (County Road 650). This becomes West Hanover Avenue at Route 202 in Morris Plains.
-Follow West Hanover Avenue; at top of hill will be County facilities on both sides.
-The Clinic is in the County Building left hand side, on the ground floor.

Parking

  • Free on premises
