Places in Washington Township, New Jersey, United States | VA Review http://www.vareview.net Tue, 02 Aug 2016 15:02:43 +0000 en-US hourly 1 Gloucester County, NJ Community Based Outpatient Clinic http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/new-jersey/washington-township/outpatient-clinic/gloucester-county-nj-community-based-outpatient-clinic/ http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/new-jersey/washington-township/outpatient-clinic/gloucester-county-nj-community-based-outpatient-clinic/#reviews Thu, 28 Jul 2016 01:58:35 +0000 http://www.vareview.net/?post_type=gd_place&p=1826 read more]]> The Community Based Outpatient Clinic located in Sewell, NJ provides primary care services for Veterans in Southern New Jersey by highly qualified primary care providers, with specialty care referrals to the Philadelphia VA Medical Center.

It also provides behavioral health services including individual, group and family counseling, and programs such as QuitSmart Smoking Cessation.

Speciality Clinics operating at this location include: Optometry, Audiology, Cardiology, Driver’s Rehab, Women’s Health, Social Work services, as well as blood drawing.

The clinic is handicapped accessible providing full and easy access for all Veterans.

Telehealth Services
Some services (listed below) at the Gloucester Clinic are provided through remote connection to the Philadelphia VA Medical Center. If you are in need of these services, please speak with your provider or other clinic staff for further information.

  • PADRECC (Parkinson’s Disease)
  • Insomnia
  • Sleep Apnea
  • Diabetic Retinal Screening
  • Dermatology Imaging
  • Social Work
  • Genetic Counseling (coming soon)
