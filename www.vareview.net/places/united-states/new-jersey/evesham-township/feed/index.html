  • Current services will continue to be available in the new location:
    • Primary Care
    • Behavioral Health
    • Social Work
    •  Podiatry
    • Women’s Health
    • Cardiology
    • Laboratory
    • Sleep Medicine
  • Following Telehealth services will be available:
    • MOVE! Nutrition
    • PADRECC
    • Insomnia
    • Dermatology
    • Teleretinal
  • New location will allow space to add the following services:
    • Optometry
    • Audiology
    • Radiology
