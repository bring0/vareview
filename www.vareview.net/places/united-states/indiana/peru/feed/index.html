Features

  • Primary care services for veterans in Peru and surrounding area, including a Clinical Pharmacist and Dietician on site for patient education needs.
  • Mental Health services including individual and group counseling such as Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) and Substance Abuse Treatment Program (SATP).
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to VA Medical Centers in Marion, Fort Wayne and Indianapolis, Indiana and Ann Arbor Michigan.
  • Laboratory: Blood drawing services available.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail. Emergent medications will be able to be filled at designated local pharmacies.

    Parking
    Free on premises
