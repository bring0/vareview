It is located at 2200 John R. Wooden Drive.

We are open Monday through Friday 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. except federal holidays.

The clinic does not offer emergency or inpatient services.

The clinic offers the following services:

  • Primary Care
  • Tele-Health
  • Orthotics
  • Phlebotomy 7:30 a.m. – 2:00pm
  • Physical Therapy
  • Mental Health Services
  • Nutrition CVT  services

Patients must be assigned to the Martinsville CBOC to receive primary care services. Please see your primary care team.

The Veterans Transportation Service can help transport ambulatory Veteran patients that have no other means of transportation to the VA medical facilities – you can get more information about transportation from the VTS office, (317) 988-2472
