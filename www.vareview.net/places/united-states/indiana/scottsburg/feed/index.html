  • Primary care services for veterans in the Scottsburg, Indiana area.
  • The center is staffed with highly trained providers specializing in providing a full range of primary care, mental health and other preventative and ancillary services.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the Robley Rex VA Medical Center.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Driving Directions

  1. Take I-65 to exit 29 for Indiana 56 toward Salem/Scottsburg.
  2. Turn right onto W McClain Ave.
  3. Turn left onto N Gardner St.
  4. Turn left onto W Moonglo Rd (right after the Scott County Hospital).
  5. Turn left onto Scott Valley Dr.
  6. The VA Healthcare Center, Scottsburg is on the right across from the County EMS.

Parking

Free on premises

Hours

Monday through Friday: 8:00 a.m. – 4:30 p.m.
