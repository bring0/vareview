It is located in Terre Haute at 110 West Honey Creek Parkway.  A VA mental health clinic is also located nearby.

We are open Monday through Friday 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.. The clinic is closed on federal holidays.

The clinic does not offer emergency or inpatient services.

We offer the following services:

  • Primary Care
  • Tele-Health
  • Orthotics
  • Phlebotomy   7:30 a.m. – 12:00 p.m.
  • General radiology

Patients must be assigned to the Terre Haute CBOC to receive primary care services. Please speak with your primary care team.

The Veterans Transportation Service can help transport ambulatory Veteran patients that have no other means of transportation to the VA medical facilities – you can get more information about transportation from the VTS office, (317) 988-2472.
