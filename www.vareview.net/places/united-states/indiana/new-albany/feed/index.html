Driving Directions

The VA Healthcare Center, New Albany is conveniently located off Grant Line Road in New Albany, Indiana, just south of I-265. To find the clinic, take either I-64 or I-65 to the I-265 Exit in Southern Indiana. Travel on I-265 to Exit 3, Hwy 111 (Grant Line Road). From the exit, turn South and immediately move into the left lane.

Just beyond the interstate overpass and prior to the next stop light, turn left onto Northgate Blvd. A cluster of new buildings include a bank, eye care center, and hand care center. The VA Healthcare Center at New Albany, also in a newly constructed building, is located directly behind these.
