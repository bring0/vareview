Reminder:  VA Choice First Program Starts with Your Provider

Recently a Peoria newspaper ran a story about OSF Healthcare announcing its participation in the VA Choice Provider Program. The news report also stated that, “For local veterans, the announcement means receiving certain types of medical care will be less likely to require traveling to a Department of Veterans Affairs facility, or have to wait for an appointment at a clinic serving populations over a wide region.”

Please note that nothing in the VA Choice First program has changed. Veterans can utilize the Choice First if the care is not available within the VA. This requires a VA provider to enter a consult to set up care with a community provider.

We are open Monday through Friday 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.. The clinic is closed on federal holidays.

The clinic does not offer emergency or inpatient services.

  • Primary Care
  • Tele-Health
  • Orthotics

Driving Directions

The VA Healthcare Center, New Albany is conveniently located off Grant Line Road in New Albany, Indiana, just south of I-265. To find the clinic, take either I-64 or I-65 to the I-265 Exit in Southern Indiana. Travel on I-265 to Exit 3, Hwy 111 (Grant Line Road). From the exit, turn South and immediately move into the left lane.

Just beyond the interstate overpass and prior to the next stop light, turn left onto Northgate Blvd. A cluster of new buildings include a bank, eye care center, and hand care center. The VA Healthcare Center at New Albany, also in a newly constructed building, is located directly behind these.
