Open Monday through Friday 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. The clinic is closed federal holidays.  Phone: (812) 336-5723

The clinic does not offer emergency or inpatient services.

The clinic offers the following services:

  • Phlebotomy services  7:30 a.m. – 1:00 p.m.
  • General Radiology
  • Primary Care
  • Tele-health
  • Orthotics

Veterans can be registered on-site and appointments scheduled.  Patients must be assigned to the Bloomington CBOC to receive primary care services.

The Veterans Transportation Service can help transport ambulatory Veteran patients that have no other means of transportation to the VA medical facilities – you can get more information about transportation from the VTN office, (317) 988-2472
