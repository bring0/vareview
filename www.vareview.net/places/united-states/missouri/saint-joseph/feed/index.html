  • Primary care services for veterans
  • Behavioral Health services
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the VA Medical Center
  • Laboratory: Blood drawing services available
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.
  • Psychiatry Services

Parking

  • Free on premises

Driving Directions

From I-29 take exit 44.  Turn west onto South Belt Highway (US-169) and travel approximately 1.2 miles. The clinic will be on the left side of street in the St. Joseph Plaza.
