  • Primary care services for Veterans in the Cape Girardeau area.
  • Behavioral Health services including individual and group thearapy, and programs such as Smoking Cessation and TeleMed services
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all Veterans.
  • Personal Care: highly-qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the John J. Pershing VA Medical Center.
  • Laboratory: blood drawing services available.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or online via: My HealtheVet.

Driving Directions

From I-55 south take Exit 96 for MO-K toward Gordonville/Cape Girardeau. Keep right at the fork, follow signs for Cape Girardeau and merge onto MO-K/William.

 

From I-55 North take Exit 96 toward Cape Girardeau  0.2 miles.
Turn left at MO-K/MO-Y/William St.
