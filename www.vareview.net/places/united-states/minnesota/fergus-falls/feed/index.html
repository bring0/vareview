  • Primary care services for veterans in the Fergus Falls metro area.
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the Fargo VA Medical Center.
  • Laboratory: Blood drawing services available.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail, My HealtheVet, or by telephone.

Other services available include:

  • Social Work
  • Telemedicine / Teleaudiology

Driving Directions

From I-94 take County Road-88.  County Road-88 becomes US-59 BR.  Stay straight to go onto W FIR AVE.  Turn left onto N Park Street.  End at 1839 N Park St.

Parking

  • Free on premises.

Hours

Monday – Friday
8:00 a.m. – 4:30 p.m.

Excluding Federal Holidays
