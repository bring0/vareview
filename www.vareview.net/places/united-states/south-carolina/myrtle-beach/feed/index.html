Features

Located on the former Myrtle Beach Air Force Base, this clinic offers 16 treatment rooms, one of which can be used for isolation.

  • Provides primary care, mental health and digital radiology.
  • No urgent or emergent care is available at this clinic.
  • Serves more than 7,000 patients in Horry County and surrounding communities.
  • Distance from Charleston VA: 83 miles.

Parking

  • Free on premises

Directions

From Charleston, SC

  • US-17 North toward Mt. Pleasant/Myrtle Beach
  • Turn slight RIGHT onto Farrow Pkwy.
  • Turn LEFT onto Phillis Blvd.
  • Myrtle Beach VA Outpatient Clinic is on the RIGHT

From Conway, SC

  • US-501 South toward Myrtle Beach
  • Merge onto Hwy. 17 Bypass South/US-17 South toward Georgetown/Surfside
  • Turn slight LEFT onto Farrow Pkwy.
  • Turn LEFT onto Phillis Blvd.
  • Myrtle Beach VA Outpatient Clinic is on the RIGHT
