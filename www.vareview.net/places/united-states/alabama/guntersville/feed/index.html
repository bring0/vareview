  • Services: Primary care and Mental Health Services.
  • Hours of operation: 7:30 – 4:30 Monday – Friday.
  • Handicapped Accessible: Full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Distance to Birmingham VAMC: 72 miles
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Driving Directions

From the Birmingham VA Medical Center:

  1. Go I-59 North to Attalla, EXIT 183.
  2. Turn LEFT at light and go to 431 North.
  3. Take 431 North to Guntersville past Lowe’s.
  4. Turn RIGHT on Applebee’s Lane to stop sign. (next to Applebee’s Restaurant).
  5. Turn RIGHT at stop sign then take an immediate LEFT. You will see the VA sign on the LEFT.
  6. Go to the end of the road and turn RIGHT onto Judy Smith Drive. Clinic will be on the RIGHT.

Parking

  • Free on premises

Hours:
8:00 am – 4:30 pm
Monday – Friday
