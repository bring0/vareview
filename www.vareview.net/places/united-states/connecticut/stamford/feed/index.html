This includes care for immediate and chronic health concerns, preventive medical care, physical exams, diagnostic laboratory testing, general X-rays and limited immediate pharmacy services.

ADVANTAGES

  • Veterans will have one primary care provider to coordinate care on an ongoing basis.
  • Veterans can receive primary medical care closer to home.
  • Veterans will still be eligible for specialty outpatient care, inpatient care and all other entitlements through the West Haven or Newington Campuses.

HOW TO ENROLL

Please mail a completed application along with a copy of your DD-214 and discharge papers to:

VA Connecticut Healthcare System
950 Campbell Avenue
West Haven, CT 06517
Attention: Eligibility Office

DIRECTIONS

Traveling south on I-95:Drive south on I-95 toward New York City. Take Exit 9 (US-1/State Hwy 106) toward Glenbrook. Turn right at East Main Street/US-1 (signs for Glenbrook). Continue to follow US-1. Turn right at CT-137/Washington Blvd. Turn right at 2nd Street/Second Street. Turn right at Summer Street. Clinic will be on your left.

From NYC and traveling north on I-95: : Take I-95 north toward New Haven. Take Exit 7 for Greenwich Avenue toward State Hwy 137 North. Merge onto McCullough Stree/South State Street. Turn left at CT-137/Washington Blvd. Turn right at 2nd Street. Turn right at Summer Street. Clinic will be on your left.

From Hartford and points north: Take I-91 South to Exit 17 (E Main St/State Hwy 15 S) toward the Wilbur Cross Parkway. Keep left at the fork to continue toward CT-15 South and merge onto CT-15 South. Take Exit 35 toward Stamford. Merge onto CT-137/High Ridge Road. Continue to follow High Ridge Road (about 2.8 miles) which will turn into Summer Street. Continue onto Summer Street. Clinic will be on your left.
