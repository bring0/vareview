The Polacca Primary Care Telehealth Outpatient Clinic (PTOC) is designed to bring primary care services to Veterans through use of telehealth technology.

Services Available:

• Disease Information
• Eligibility and Enrollment
• Health Maintenance
• Health Promotion
• Health Screening
• Laboratory (HHCC)
• EKG’s
• Management of Acute and Chronic Disease
• Mental Health
• Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) Counseling
• Primary Care

Procedures, such as:

• Skin tests
• Dressings changes
• Immunizations

 

Hours of Operation:

  • 8:00 a.m. – 4:30 p.m.

  • Toll Free 1-800-949-1005, ext. 4465
  • Telicare is 1-800-581-8387 for after hour’s health care advice
