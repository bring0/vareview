The Flagstaff Community Based Outpatient Clinic (CBOC) is designed to bring primary care services to Veterans living in Coconino County.

  • Services and Scope of Care:

    • Disease Information
    • Eligibility and Enrollment
    • Health Maintenance
    • Health Promotion
    • Health Screening
    • Laboratory
    • Management of Acute and Chronic Diseases
    • Mental Health
    • Primary Care
    • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
    • Diabetic Retinal Eye Exam
    • EKGs
    • Tele dermatology
    • Routine/Urgent Care Procedures, such as:
    • Skin tests
    • Dressing changes
    • Immunizations
    • Women’s Health

    8:00 a.m. – 4:30 p.m. Monday – Friday
    (closed Federal Holidays)
    Local Phone: (928) 226-1056
    Toll Free 1-800-949-1005, ext. 7820

Location:

  • 1300 West University Avenue #200
    Flagstaff, AZ  86001

Driving Directions: From I-17N to Flagstaff, Arizona

  • Take Lake Mary Road Exit #339, toward Mormon Lake
  • Turn left onto S. Lake Mary Road
  • Turn right onto AZ-89-Alt N
  • Turn left onto S. Woodlands Village Blvd.
  • Turn left onto W. University Avenue
  • The VA Clinic is on the right

Driving directions:  From I-40W

  • Take I-17 exit 195 toward AZ-89A N/Flagstaff/US-180N/Grand Canyon
  • Turn left onto S. Beaulah Blvd.
  • Take the 2nd right onto S. Woodlands Village Blvd.
  • Turn left onto w. University Avenue
  • The VA Clinic is on the right

Other Pertinent Information:

  • No emergency care or walk-in appointments are available at this clinic.
