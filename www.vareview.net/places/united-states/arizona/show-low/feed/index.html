This clinic is based on a primary care model of health care that has a physician and nurse practitioners who provide care..  The clinic has full-time social workers, a part-time podiatrist, a laboratory draw station and several modalities of TeleHealth.  There is a full-time Arizona Department of Veteran Services representative onsite. The clinic does not have a pharmacy onsite, prescriptions will be filled by mail through the main facility. Veterans are seen by appointment only.

Appointments

If you have a medical emergency, call 911.

Medical visits are by appointment only.

Your health care provider will schedule appointments as needed.

If you are ill or have questions about your care, you are encouraged to call the VA Help Line, which is available 24 hours a day/7 days a week at (602) 222-6550,  toll-free in Arizona at 1-888-214-7264.

Please call to cancel any appointment you cannot keep. This allows us to schedule other patients who may be ill.

To cancel an appointment, call the VA Help Line, which is available 24 hours a day/7 days a week, at (602) 222-6550, toll-free in Arizona at 1-888-214-7264.

To make your appointments more productive, we suggest that you arrive 30 minutes before your appointment and bring with you:

  • All medications.
  • Home blood pressure records.
  • Diabetes diaries.
  • All outside medical records including those from other VA facilities.
  • Any other information that may apply to your care.

Driving Directions

From US 60
Turn south onto N. White Mountain Road. Turn right onto Cub Lake Road. End at 5171 Cub Lake Road.

From Pinetop-Lakeside
Head northwest on E. White Mountain Boulevard. Turn left onto Cub Lake Road, End at 5171 Cub Lake Road.

Parking – Free on premises.
