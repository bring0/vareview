The Cottonwood Community Based Outpatient Clinic (CBOC) is designed to bring primary care services to Veterans living in Eastern Yavapai County.

Services and Scope of Care:

  • Disease Information
  • Eligibility and Enrollment
  • Health Maintanance
  • Health Promotion
  • Health Screening
  • Laboratory
  • Management of Acute and Chronic Diseases
  • Mental Health
  • Primary Care
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Diabetic Retinal Eye Exam
  • EKGs
  • Tele dermatology
  • Routine/Urgent Care Procedures, such as:
  • Skin tests
  • Dressing changes
  • Immunizations
  • Women’s Health

Hours of Operation:

  • 8:00 a.m. – 4:30 p.m. Monday – Friday
    (closed Saturday, Sunday and Federal Holidays)
  • Local Phone: (928) 649-1532
    Toll Free: 1-800-949-1005 extension 7331

Location:

  • 501 South Willard
    Cottonwood, AZ 86326

Other Pertinent Information:

  • No emergency care or walk-in appointments are available at this clinic.
