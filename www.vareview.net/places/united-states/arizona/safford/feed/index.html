The Safford Community Bases Outpatient Clinic (CBOC) provides Primary Care services to Veterans living in Graham, Greenlee, Northern Cochise counties of Arizona, and Hildago County, New Mexcio

Hours of Operation:
Monday through Friday 8:00 a.m. – 4:30 p.m., closed all Federal Holidays

Services and Scope of Care

• Comprehensive Primary Care: Acute and Chronic Disease Management
• Enrollment and Eligibility
• Health Screening, Education, Promotion and Maintenance
• Immunizations
• Mental Health
• Nutrition Education and Counseling by Telehealth
• Pharmacy Consultation by TeleHealth
• Social Work
• Specimen Collection Laboratory
• Telemedicine/TeleHealth Education
• Women’s Health

Eligibility:
For information about enrollment in the Safford Community Based Clinic, call Eligibility and Enrollment (928) 428-8010.

Other Pertinent Information:
For appointments or healthcare advice, call the Community Based Outpatient Clinic at (928) 428-8010 or the Southern Arizona VA Health Care System toll free at 1-800-470-8262.  To assist in your care and treatment, please bring all of your medications to your first appointment.

Emergency Care:
No Emergency Care is available at this facility. For after hours emergency care call 911.
