The Casa Grande Community Based Outpatient Clinic (CBOC) is designed to deliver Primary Care Services to eligible veterans living in Pinal county and outlying areas.

Hours of Operation:
Monday through Friday 8:00am – 4:30pm (except Federal Holidays).

Services and Scope of Care

• Audiology
• Comprehensive Primary Care: Acute and Chronic Disease Management
• Enrollment and Eligibility
• Health Screening, Education, Promotion and Maintenance
• Immunizations
• Mental Health
• Nutrition Education and Counseling
• Optometry
• Physical Therapy
• Pharmacy Consultation
• Social Work
• Specimen Collection Laboratory
• Women’s Health
• X-Ray

Eligibility
For information about enrollment in the Casa Grande CBOC call (520) 836-2536.

Other Pertinent Information:
For appointments or healthcare advice, call the Community Based Outpatient Clinic at (520) 836-2536 or the Southern Arizona VA Health Care System toll free at 1-800-470-8262.
To assist in your care and treatment, please bring all of your medications to your first appointment.

Emergency Care:
No Emergency Care is available at this facility. For after hours emergency care call 911.
