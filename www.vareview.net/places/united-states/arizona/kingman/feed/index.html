The Kingman Community Based Outpatient Clinic (CBOC) is designed to bring primary care services to Veterans living in Mohave County.

Services and Scope of Care:

  • Disease Information
  • Eligibility and Enrollment
  • Home Based Primary Care
  • Health Promotion
  • Homeless Program
  • Laboratory
  • Management of Acute and Chronic Diseases
  • Mental Health
  • Primary Care
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Diabetic Retinal Eye Exam
  • EKGs
  • Physical Therapy
  • Tele dermatology
  • Routine/Urgent Care Procedures, such as:
  • Skin tests
  • Dressing changes
  • Immunizations
  • Women’s Health

Hours of Operation:

  • 8:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Monday – Friday
    (closed Federal Holidays)
    Local Phone: (928) 718-7300
    Toll Free: 1-800-949-1005 ext. 7300

Location:

  • 2668 Hualapai Mountain Road
    Kingman, AZ 86401Other Pertinent Information:

No emergency care or walk-in appointments are available at this clinic.
