The Tuba City Primary Care Telehealth Outpatient Clinic (PTOC) is designed to bring primary care services to Veterans living in Coconino County.

Services and Scope of Care:

  • Primary Care through the use of advancing telehealth technology
  • Mental Health/ PTSD
  • Eligibility and Enrollment
  • Health Maintenance
  • Health Promotion
  • Health Screening
  • Laboratory
  • Management of Acute and Chronic Diseases
  • Immunizations
  • Women’s Health
  • Education

Hours of Operation:

  • 8:00 a.m. – 4:30 p.m.
  • Local Phone: (928) 283-4465
  • Toll Free 1-800-949-1005, ext. 4465
  • Telicare is 1-800-581-8387 for after hour’s health care advice

Location:

  • 167 N. Main Street
    Tuba City, AZ  86045
