The Anthem Community Based Outpatient Clinic (CBOC) is designed to bring primary care services to Veterans living in Southern Yavapai and Northern Maricopa Counties.

Services and Scope of Care:

  • Dermatology
  • Education
  • Eligibility and Enrollment
  • Health Maintenance
  • Health Promotion
  • Health Screening
  • Laboratory
  • Management of Acute and Chronic Diseases
  • Mental Health
  • Primary Care
  • Routine/Urgent Care Procedures, such as:
  • Skin tests
  • Dressing changes
  • Immunizations
  • Women’s Health

Hours of Operation:

  • 8:00 a.m. – 4:30 p.m. Monday – Friday
    (closed Saturday, Sunday, and Federal Holidays)
  • Local Phone: (623) 249-2300
    Toll Free 1-800-949-1005, ext. 2300

Location:

  • Anthem Medical Plaza
    3618 West Anthem Way
    Building D, Suite 120
    Anthem, AZ 85086

Driving Directions:

  • Take I-17 to Anthem Way and turn east;
  • Take Anthem way to Gavilan Peak Parkway and turn left;
  • Turn left again at the first parking lot for CVS Pharmacy;
  • Take immediate right into the medical building parking lot;
  • The Anthem CBOC, Suite D-120, is in the corner with an American Flag at the front doorway;
  • Parking is available in the adjacent parking lot.

Other Pertinent Information:

  • No emergency care or walk-in appointments are available at this facility.
