The Holbrook Primary Care Telehealth Outpatient Clinic (PTOC) is designed to bring primary care services to Veterans through use of telehealth technology.

Services and Scope of Care:

  • Disease Information
  • Eligibility and Enrollment
  • Health Maintenance
  • Health Promotion
  • Health Screening
  • Laboratory
  • Management of Acute and Chronic Diseases
  • Mental Health
  • Primary Care
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Diabetic Retinal Eye Exam
  • EKGs
  • Tele dermatology
  • Routine/Urgent Care Procedures, such as:
  • Skin tests
  • Dressing changes
  • Immunizations
  • Women’s Health

Hours of Operation:

  • 8:00 a.m. – 4:30 p.m.
  • Local Phone: (928) 524-1050
  • Toll Free 1-800-949-1005, ext. 5292

Location:

  • 33 West Vista Drive
    Holbrook, AZ 86025
