The Payson Veterans Affairs Health Care Clinic offers primary care by a physician and support staff. The clinic does not have a pharmacy onsite, prescriptions will be filled by mail through the main facility. Patients are seen in this clinic by appointment only after their eligibility and enrollment has been confirmed.

Appointments

If you have a medical emergency, call 911.

Medical visits are by appointment only.  If you would like to enroll in the Payson Veterans Affairs Health Care Clinic, please contact (602) 222-6533. The staff will ensure your enrollment information is up-to-date, and will put you on the appointment eligibility list for this clinic.

If you are ill or have questions about your care, you are encouraged to call VA Help Line at (602) 222-6550 or toll-free in Arizona at 1-888-214-7264.

Please call to cancel any appointments you cannot keep. This allows us to schedule other patients who may be ill.

To make your appointment more productive, we suggest that you arrive 30 minutes before your appointment and bring with you:

  • All medications.
  • Home blood pressure records.
  • Diabetes diaries.
  • All outside medical records including those from other VA facilities.
  • Any other information that may apply to your care.

Driving Directions

From Strawberry,  Arizona
Head south on Highway 87 towards Payson. Turn east onto Highway 260.  Drive approximately 8/10 of a mile.  End at 903 E State Highway 260, Suite 3 (south side of highway).

From Star Valley, Arizona
Head west on Highway 260.  End at 903 E State Highway 260, Suite 3 (south side of highway).

Parking – Free on premises.
