  • Primary Care, Tele-Mental Health, Lab Testing, Immunizations and Preventive Health Services for Veterans in the Tucker County area.
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all Veterans.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Driving Directions

A: Traveling South from Oakland, Maryland

1. Take Rt. 219 S to stop light in Parson (intersection of Routes 219 and 72)
2. Turn right onto Rt. 72
3. Take 2nd left after stop light onto Third Street (VA Clinic sign on left)
4. Go 1 block to top of hill and turn left onto Spruce Street
5. VA Clinic is the brick building beside the Senior Center

B: Traveling North from Elkins, WV

1. Take Rt. 219 N to stop light in Parsons (intersection of Routes 219 and 72)
2. Proceed straight through stop light onto Rt. 72
3. Take 2nd left after stop light onto Third Street (VA Clinic sign on left)
4. Go 1 block to top of hill and turn left onto Spruce Street
5. VA Clinic is the brick building beside the Senior Center

C: Traveling from Kingwood, WV

1. Take Rt. 7 East to intersection of Rt. 72, turn right onto Rt. 72
2. Stay on Rt. 72 through Rowlesburg to Rt. 50
3. Turn right onto Rt. 50 and go approximately ½ mile to Rt. 50 and Rt. 72 split
4. Turn left onto Rt. 72 to Parsons
5. In Parsons turn right onto Third Street (VA Clinic sign on the corner on the right)
6. Go 1 block to top of hill and turn left onto Spruce Street
7. VA Clinic is the brick building beside the Senior Center

Parking

  • Free on premises
