  • Primary Care, Tele-Mental Health, Lab Testing, Immunizations and Preventive Health Services for Veterans in the Mon County area.
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all Veterans.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Driving Directions

A: Traveling West on I-68

Take I-68 West to I 79 Split, take I-79 North and follow directions below

B: Traveling North on I-79

1. Take Westover Exit #152 from I-79 North
2. Turn right at end of ramp (will see an Exxon Station on right)
3. Go up to next Stop Light (will see a Taco Bell and McDonald’s on your right)
4. Turn right between Taco Bell and McDonald’s – it is Wes Mon Plaza.
5. Drive to straight to back of Plaza (see Big Lot’s store – orange letters)
6. VA Clinic is on far right corner of the same building (beside the Enconolodge).

C: Traveling South on I-79

1. Take Westover exit #152 from I-79 North
2. Turn left at end of ramp
3. Go up to next Stop Light (will see a Taco Bell and McDonald’s on your right)
4. Go straight through the 1st stop light (will see an Exxon Station ahead on right)
5. Turn right between Taco Bell and McDonald’s – it is Wes Mon Plaza.
6. Drive to straight to back of Plaza (see Big Lot’s store – orange letters)
7. VA Clinic is on far right corner of the same building (beside the Enconolodge).

Parking

  • Free on premises
