The Petersburg clinic offers primary care with an emphasis on preventative health care.

The hours of operation, excluding federal holidays are:
Monday: 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
Tuesday: 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
Wednesday: 8 a.m. to 5:30 p.m.
Thursday: 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
Friday: 7 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.
To view the Petersburg CBOC fact sheet, click here.

Services Offered

  • Preventive Health Care and screening
  • Health Promotion
  • Annual Physicals
  • Cancer Screening
  • Management of chronic illnesses such as diabetes and hypertension
  • Immunizations
  • Mental Health services
  • Health Education
  • Advanced directives services
  • Post-hospital follow-up
  • TelePsychiatry
  • TeleSpeech
  • TelePalliative Care
  • TeleVIST
  • TelePain
  • TeleMST
  • TeleGenomics

Driving Directions

From the North:
Head southeast on WV-42
Turn left onto US-220N/Cumberland Rd
Turn left onto Grant St.

From the South:
Head north on US-220 N
Turn right continuing on US-220 N/Cumberland Rd. N
Turn left onto Grant St

From the West:
Head east until you find US-220 N/Cumberland Road N
Take a left onto US-220 N/Cumberland Road N
Turn left onto Grant St

From the East:
Head west until you find US-220 S/Cumberland Rd S
Take a left onto US-220 S/Cumberland Rd S
Turn right onto Grant St

Parking

  • Free on premises
