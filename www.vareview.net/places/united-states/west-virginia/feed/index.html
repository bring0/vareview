]]> http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/west-virginia/franklin/outpatient-clinic/franklin-community-based-outpatient-clinic/feed/ Rural Mobile Unit http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/west-virginia/clarksburg/outpatient-clinic/rural-mobile-unit/ http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/west-virginia/clarksburg/outpatient-clinic/rural-mobile-unit/#reviews Mon, 18 Jul 2016 00:48:23 +0000 http://www.vareview.net/?post_type=gd_place&p=803 read more]]> The Rural Mobile Health Unit is supported by a team of health care professionals charged to meet the following objectives:

Outreach and Screening:

The Team will focus on preventative health care screening, mental health outreach, influenza/pneumonia vaccinations and routine primary care, particularly for Veterans with difficulty accessing VA Health Care.

OEF/OIF Outreach:

The Team will partner with the West Virginia national Guard, reserve units, Camp Dawson and other military units in support of pre/post deployed Veterans. Services will include VA enrollment/benefit information, suicide prevention training/education, family counseling, primary care and mental health screening.

Community Based Outpatient Clinic (CBOC) Support and Integration:

The Team will expand the efforts of our four CBOCs in “taking services to veterans.”

Emergency/Disaster Management:

The Team will support Veterans, their families and communities during major disasters and other emergencies.

Healthier Communities:

The Team will promote the VA’s goal of encouraging healthier communities and support the VAMC’s preventative health care programs, pandemic flu initiatives, diabetes education , and smoking/tobacco cessation.
