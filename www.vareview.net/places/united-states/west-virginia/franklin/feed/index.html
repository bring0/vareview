The Franklin clinic offers primary care with an emphasis on preventative health care. The clinic hours are Monday through Thursday from 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. and Friday 7 a.m. to 2:30 p.m., excluding federal holidays. To view the Franklin Contract CBOC fact sheet, click here.

Services Offered

  • Preventive Health Care and screening
  • Health Promotion
  • Annual Physicals
  • Cancer Screening
  • Management of chronic illnesses such as diabetes and hypertension
  • Immunizations
  • Mental Health services
  • Health Education services
  • Telehealth services
  • Social Work services

Driving Directions

  1. Interstate 81 to exit for Route 33 West at Harrisonburg, Va.
  2. Follow Route 33 to Franklin, W.Va.
  3. At the stop light in Franklin, turn left onto Route 220 South
  4. Turn right on Pine Street
  5. The Franklin VA Community Based Outpatient Clinic is located on the left

Parking

  • Free on premises
