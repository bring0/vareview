  • Primary care services on an outpatient basis, as well as limited specialty care services available through Telehealth conferencing with providers at the Huntington VA Medical Center.
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the Huntington VA Medical Center.
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet. All medications prescribed are in accordance with VA guidelines.

Schedule/Re-schedule/Cancel an Appointment

To schedule, re-schedule or cancel an appointment, Veterans may contact the VA Medical Center toll free at 800-827-8244, extension 7121. Veterans may also contact the Lenore Clinic directly at 304-475-3000.

Parking

Ample free parking on premises
