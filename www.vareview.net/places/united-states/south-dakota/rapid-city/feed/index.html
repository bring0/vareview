  • Primary Care services for veterans in the Rapid City metro area.
  • Handicapped accessible for full and easy access to all veterans.
  • Personal Care:  Highly qualilfied Primary Care providers, specialty care referrals to the VA Black Hills Health Care System, Fort Meade and Hot Springs campuses.
  • Laboratory offers blood drawing services from 7:00 a.m. to 9:00 a.m., Monday through Friday.
  • Prescriptions are processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Driving Directions:
From Interstate 90 – Exit 57 West Boulevard to Omaha Street.  Turn left for 2 blocks to Mount Rushmore Road (US 16).  Turn right on Mount Rushmore and go north 1.4 miles to Cathedral Drive.  Follow Cathedral Drive 0.3 miles and turn right on 5th Street for 0.6 miles.  The clinic is located on the right side of the street at 3525 5th Street.   Parking is free on premises.
