  • Primary Care services for veterans in the Pine Ridge area.
  • Behavioral Health services including Compensated Work Therapy program serving the veterans of Ogallala and Rosebud Nations.
  • Handicapped accessible offering full and easy access to all veterans.
  • Personal Care:  Highly qualified Primary Care providers, specialty care referrals to the VA Black Hills Health Care System, Fort Meade and Hot Springs Campuses.
  • Prescriptions are processed through the mail or by My HealtheVet.

Driving Directions:
Located next to the Indian Health Service on East Highway 18 in Pine Ridge, SD.
Free parking on premises.
