  • Primary care services for veterans in the Denver Metro, Front Range and Mountain communities.
  • Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans with parking spots located near the front entrance to the clinic
  • Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the Denver VA Medical Center.
  • Laboratory & Radiology services are available at this clinic.
  • Physical Therapy: A wide range of Physical Therapy services will be offered at the clinic. You must have a Physical Therapy consult from your VA provider to receive therapy.
  • Other Services available include Nutritional Medicine, Audiology, and Travel Vouchers.
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail, in the community via Heritage Pharmacy, or My HealtheVet.
  • Compensation & Pension: This clinic is designed to support this program by having audiology, laboratory and radiological services necessary for these evaluations.

The new clinic is a tobacco-free campus which includes e-cigarettes. Please help us make this move to an improved health environment by not using tobacco in any form on the campus, including the parking lot.

Driving Directions

From US-6 W / W 6th Ave turn right onto Johnson Road. Follow Johnson Road north to 10th Avenue. The Golden VA Clinic will be on the right-hand sign, in the center building of the office complex.

Parking

  • Ample handicap and regular patient parking is provided both in front of the clinic and to the east.

Hours

  • Primary Care and Mental Health clinic (Second floor) hours of operation – Monday through Friday 7:30 am to 4:30 pm.
  • Compensation and Pension Medical Exam section (First floor) will be open Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM, and Saturdays from 7:30 am to 4:30 pm.
  • Laboratory, Radiology, Physical Therapy, and other ancillary departments will be open from 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM. The clinic will be closed all Federal Holidays.
  • Please call the clinic at 303-914-2680 for appointments, and the Nurse Information Call Center at 303-399-8020 then option 2 for questions about your medical care. After hours this number will be answered by triage nurses who will review your reported concern/issue and make appropriate referral for healthcare.
