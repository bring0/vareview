2001 70th Avenue
Suite 200
Greeley, CO 80634

Phone numbers for the clinic are (970) 313-0027 or (888) 481-4065.

Community-Based Outpatient Clinic: Features

Primary care services for veterans in the Greeley, Colorado area.
Handicapped Accessible: full and easy access for all veterans.
Personal Care: Highly qualified primary care providers, specialty care referrals to the Cheyenne VA Medical Center.
Laboratory: Blood drawing services available.
Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Driving Directions

From Highway 34, turn north onto 71st Avenue. Continue north until intersecting with West 20th Street.

Turn east onto West 20th Street and make the first right into the “Summit View Medical Commons” building complex.

From downtown Greeley, head west on West 20th Street. Immediately before going through the intersection of West 20th Street and 71st Avenue, turn left onto 70th Avenue. There is a large sign for the “Summit View Medcial Commons” building complex where this left turn needs to be made.

Parking

Free on premises
