The Rawlins VA PCTOC features:

  • On-going coordination of your overall medical care, including:
  • Wellness promotion and immunizations
  • Periodic appropriate health screening procedures
  • Management of chronic illnesses
  • Referral to specialty clinics, as needed
  • Mental Health and well being Exams
  • Individual Counseling
  • Lab and hematology
  • EKG’s
  • Tele-retinal imaging

Laboratory: Blood drawing services available.
Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Scheduling / Canceling Appointments:

Scheduling your appointment:   1-888-483-9127 Ext. 3816 or Ext. 7952
Or visit our clinic at 1809 E. Daley St. in Rawlins, WY
Bring the following to your appointments:

  •  VA ID Card
  •  Medications or medication bottles (including herbals, “over the counter” and non-VA meds)
  •  Documentation from recent non-VA hospitalization / Procedures

Canceling your Appointments:  1-888-483-9127 Ext 3816 or Ext. 7952

Specialty Appointments:

The scope of care that the Telehealth Clinic is designed to deliver is for on-going, preventative care appointments. For consults to VA’s Specialty Clinics, you will have a consult to be seen at the VA Medical Center in Cheyenne, WY.
