Driving Directions

General Location

The new Southeast Primary Care Clinic is located on the EAST side of South Boulder Hwy., between Horizon Dr./Racetrack Road. and College Drive.

Coming from East:

Heading SOUTH on I-515, take the Horizon Drive Exit, #59. Make a LEFT ontoHorizon Dr. Turn LEFT on South Boulder Hwy/NV-582. The Southeast Clinic will be on your RIGHT. If you’ve reached College Dr., you’ve gone a little too far. Make a u-turn heading, SOUTH on Boulder Hwy. Make a LEFT at San Jacinto Road and into the clinic parking lot.

Coming from the South:

Head NORTH on US-93, exit onto Boulder Hwy, exit #56B, heading NORTH. The Southeast Clinic will be on your RIGHT. If you’ve reached College Dr., you’ve gone a little too far. Make a u-turn heading SOUTH on Boulder Hwy. Make a LEFT at San Jacinto Road and into the clinic parking lot.

Coming from the North:

Head SOUTH on US-95. Take the Horizon Dr. exit #59. Turn LEFT on S. Boulder Hwy/NV-582. The Southeast Clinic will be on your RIGHT. If you’ve reached College Drive., you’ve gone a little too far. Make a u-turn headingSOUTH on Boulder Hwy. Make a LEFT at San Jacinto Road. and into the clinic parking lot.

Coming from the West:

Head EAST on I-215. Take the NV-564/Lake Mead Pkwy exit on LEFT and remain on Lake Mead Pkwy for about two miles. Take a slight RIGHT onto South Boulder Hwy. Make a LEFT at San Jacinto Road and into the clinic parking lot.
