Features

  • Primary Care – Emphasis on Prevention Management of Chronic Illnesses
  • Wide range of Mental Healh Care Options, to include individual and group therpay for PTSD and Substance Abse
  • Additional Services to includes EKG/Holter Monitoring, Phlebotomy, Audiology, Optometry, Diabetic Shoe Clinic, and Respiratory Services.
  • Additional Services available through Telemedicine – Physical Therapy, MOVE Program, Teleretinal, Diabetic Education, and Nutrition Assistance.
  • Veteran ID Cards
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Parking

  • Free on premises.
