The Quad Cities Outpatient Clinic is an extension of the Iowa City VA Health Care System. The clinic has five permanent physicians, a clinical pharmacists, and a support staff of nurses, health care technicians, and administrative specialists. In addition, a dietitian, a social worker, and others travel from the Iowa City VA Health Care System to oversee the medical needs of veterans. The clinic is conveniently located in Bettendorf, Iowa. The clinic operates like a doctor’s office, not an emergency center, providing primary care, preventative health, and wellness services. A specific healthcare team is assigned to address your healthcare needs. You may be referred to Iowa City for specialty treatment. It is required that you call ahead for an appointment.

The Clinic also provides X-ray, EKG, lab, and pharmacy services. Patient education includes substance abuse counseling, smoking cessation classes, and nutrition counseling. Physical Therapy services are available on Tuesdays and Thursdays.

Driving Directions

From I-74 take Spruce Hills Drive to 18th Street and turn left. Go one block to Tech Drive and turn right. The clinic is located at the next corner which is the intersection of Victoria Street and Tech Drive.
