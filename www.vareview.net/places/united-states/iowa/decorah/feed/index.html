The Decorah Outpatient Clinic is an extension of the Iowa City VA Health Care System. The clinic is conveniently located in Decorah, Iowa. The clinic operates like a doctor’s office, NOT an emergency center, providing primary care, preventative health, and wellness services. A specific healthcare team is assigned to address your healthcare needs. You may be referred to the Iowa City VA Health Care System for specialty treatment. It is required that you call ahead for an appointment.
