The Waterloo Outpatient Clinic is an outpatient facility where veterans can be treated for medical problems and routine health maintenance. The clinic operates like a doctor’s office, NOT an emergency center. Services provided by the Waterloo Outpatient Clinic include physician evaluations, basic x-rays, routine laboratory testing, and EKGs (electrocardiograms), primary care, preventative health, and wellness services. A specific healthcare team is assigned to address your healthcare needs. You may be referred to Iowa City for specialty treatment. It is required that you call ahead for an appointment. Initial supplies of newly prescribed medications are available through a local pharmacy. Refills will be mailed from the Iowa City VA Health Care System.

The Ottumwa Outpatient Clinic is an extension of the Iowa City VA Health Care System. The clinic is conveniently located in Ottumwa, Iowa. The clinic operates like a doctor’s office, NOT an emergency center, providing primary care, preventative health, and wellness services. A specific healthcare team is assigned to address your healthcare needs. You may be referred to the Iowa City VA Health Care System for specialty treatment. It is required that you call ahead for an appointment.

The Clinic also provides X-ray, EKG, lab, and pharmacy services. Patient education includes Substance Abuse Counseling, Stop Smoking Classes, and Nutrition Counseling.

Driving Directions

The Ottumwa Outpatient Clinic is located near the Ottumwa Regional Medical Center at 1009 East Pennsylvania Ave., Ottumwa, Iowa 52501
