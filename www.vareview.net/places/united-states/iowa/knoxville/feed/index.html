Places in Knoxville, Iowa, United States | VA Review http://www.vareview.net Tue, 02 Aug 2016 15:02:43 +0000 en-US hourly 1 Knoxville CBOC http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/iowa/knoxville/outpatient-clinic/knoxville-cboc/ http://www.vareview.net/places/united-states/iowa/knoxville/outpatient-clinic/knoxville-cboc/#reviews Fri, 08 Jul 2016 01:04:58 +0000 http://www.vareview.net/?post_type=gd_place&p=461 read more]]> VA is pleased to announce that on September 30, 2015, it awarded a lease contract for the 12,835 net usable square foot (nusf) Knoxville CBOC to JTW Development, for a lease term of 15 years. The CBOC will be located in a new building located on the site of Fairlane Bowl at 1607 N. Lincoln Street, Knoxville, Iowa 50138.

The CBOC, with 105 parking spaces, will allow VA to provide timely access to Primary Care, Audiology, Podiatry, Optometry, and Mental Health services to Veterans in Knoxville and the surrounding areas. It is anticipated that the CBOC’s construction will be completed in the Spring of 2017, with doors opening to Veterans after the Summer of 2017.

The current CBOC is located at 1515 West Pleasant Street (Building 1) and offers a variety of services to local Veterans.  The Department of Veterans Affairs Office of Asset and Enterprise Management located in Washington, DC continues to have a lease agreement of the historical Knoxville campus with Knoxville Veterans Alliance (KVA) while they consider options to develop the land under the enhanced use lease process.

Features

  • Primary Ambulatory Care
  • Mental Health
  • Prescriptions: Routine prescriptions processed through the mail or My HealtheVet.

Driving Directions

From Interstate 80. Take Exit 164 turn toward Monroe on Highway 14. Travel 29.9 miles to Knoxville. Turn right on Pleasant Street. Travel .1 miles. The clinic is located on your right.

Parking

  • Free on premises.
