The Dubuque Outpatient Clinic is an extension of the Iowa City VA Health Care System. The clinic is conveniently located in Dubuque, Iowa. The clinic operates like a doctor’s office, not an emergency center, providing primary care, preventative health, and wellness services. A specific healthcare team is assigned to address your healthcare needs. You may be referred to the Iowa City VA Health Care System for specialty treatment. It is required that you call ahead for an appointment.

Driving Directions

The Dubuque Outpatient Clinic is located at Mercy Health Center, 250 Mercy Drive, Dubuque, Iowa. Take the Hill/Bryant Exit off Dodge Street.

Parking
